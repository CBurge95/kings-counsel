Att saint James the seconde day off
April being than present as the day
before

Thomas wiatt and yong b pikering being eftsones
examined of thoffenses abousesaide towching the walking in
the streetes wyth stone bowes / the saide pikering stil continued
in his os obstinate denying off thesame / vntyll suche tyme as
Clere whiche had ben before examined avowed itt was vnto his
to the towre / And afterward yong wiatt confessing frankly
the hole matter and going abowght to excuse his obstinate
deniall off thesame de the day before was likewyse aswell
for the matter as his sayde obstinacye committed likewyse
vnto the towre /


Att saint James the third day off Aprill
being than present
Tharchebisshop off Canterbury
The lorde chawncellour off Englond
The he lorde privy seale
Therle off hertford
The lord saint John
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
Sir John gage Comptroller
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsses
Sir Antony wingfelde vicechamberlain
Sir thomas wriothesley secretary

Jhon Butlar seruant to the lorde william vpon suspition
of the concealement of the convayance off certeyne gowlde was
committed to the porters warde /

John Booles hauing ben bownde as before appereth to
deliuer a certayne pinesse taken by him vpon the see
and to make full recompense of the spoyle thereoff to one
spaniard / this day browght in before the
cownsell a testimoniall of thaccomplisshement off the order
appoynted vnto him on that behalff /
