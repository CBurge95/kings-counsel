Wheras a lettere was addressed to John Smyth one of
the kinges watermen / to delyver vnto John Anthony
certain katches and in then C quarters of wheate had
seased for a forfait certain katches and in them C quarters of
wheate belonging to John Anthony of Kent / and the saide
Anthony cha was bounde by oblig Forasmuch the saide mater
was in suspense and the said Anthony had founde here suretyes
he wer condempred vppon the same / A lettere was written
to the said Smyth to delyver the saide Catches and corne
vnto the saide Anthony which he refusyng to do and receyving
the said letteres vnreverently was sent for and committed to warde
and a li an other of like effect was written to the Custumers
of London /
