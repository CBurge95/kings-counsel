portugallois / other to cawse ther gooddes to be restored vnot them

Att hamptoncourt the xvth day off
July being present as the day before

The condition of the recognsiance as if thabouebounden Evangelista
Michaeli and Jheronimo do dewly and trewly deliuer for the
kinges behalff all suche gooddes as being in theyre possession shall
clerely be knowen betwene this and candlemas to apperteyne to frenchemen / and farther do
abyde all suche ordres for that that is dowghtfull and ambigus
ambiguows as shall be taken by the cownsell / and do present theyre
sayde parsons at the sayde feast off candlemas vnto the
cownsell than etc. orells etc. /

An ordre was taken betwene


Apud hamptoncourte xvj Mensus Julij

Where as a matter of contention was exibited vnto the Counsill betwene the
Baylifes and Fremen of Kingson vpon thames on the one partie and
thenhabitantes of Northbeton on the other partie for and concerning
certeyne Wooddes growing vpon the Commvn of the saide northbeton
For asmuche as the saide baylifes and Freemen of kingston have
shewed and declared that the saide wooddes apperteine to the kinges
royalte the graunte whereof they have in theyre fee ferme
and that as they haue hitherto vsed so they woll and determyn to
putt the saide Wooddes to non other vse that to the mending and
repayring and the entreteyning of the wayes / and that the saide
inhabitauntes of Northebeten can shewe no speciall mattier to
Lett of inhibite the saide Baylifes and Freemen of kingston on
this behalf : It is ordered and decreed that the saide Baylifes
and freemen shall aswell cary awaye the Woodde alredy felled
inthe saide Commun of Northbeton as fell also from tyme to
tyme for the purposes above saide onely / all suche wodde as
to them shall therefore be thought good and expedient vntill such
tyme as the said inhabitauntes of Northbeton shalbe able
to shewe Sufficient matter for the contrary

Whereas a matter off contention and variaunce hath long depended
betwene therle off Ourmonde and James Esquier
