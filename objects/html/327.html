Letters wer receyued from the lorde deputie wyth aduertisement
that vpon charge and commawndement was gyven to all capitans and
others wythin bullouoine and picardie to be readye wythin oon howres
warning /

Lettres wer deliured from sir Jhon walloppe wyth aduertisement
that the commen bruwte was in fraunce that the duke off Najara sholde
coom ambassadour from themperour to the frenche king /

A warrant was vndre the stampe was directed to syr richard
grasham for the deliuerance off thre hundreth duckettes to thomas
chawmberlayne / whome at this present the kinges highnes sent
in to portugall for the triall off certayne parsons co vehement
lye suspected to be iuis / the gooddes off whome wer committed
in the meane season to the custodye of the sayde sir richard
gressham


Att westminster the xvth day off Marche
being than present as the day before

Letters wer deuised ans and signed with the cownselles handes
declaring the order taken here to the president and cownsell
off the marches off wales / the order taken betwene Jhon price
secretarie wyth the sayde cownsell / and Edmonde and Charles
fox clerckes off the signet there / whiche was the custodye off
the signett to be deliuered to Jhon price or to his sufficient deputie immediately as apper
teyning to his office off secretarye / And the sayde president
and the cownsell to appoynt summ convenient personage for thexcerci
sing off the clerkship off the signett in thabsence off s the sayde
foxis / sequestring in to theyre owne handes all fees and dewtyes
as nowgh rise or grow of the same / vntill suche tyme as a finall
determination therin might be taken here /
