highnes commissioners appoynted to stay all frenchemennes gooddes /
fo the deliuerye toff certeyne other gooddes stayed as apperteyning

to frenchemen /

Steven allemaigne hauing hadde charge long tyme
off certeyne off the kinges highnes buisldinges and fortifications /
forasmuche as itt appered he hadde lewdelye behaued himselff
in the sayde charge and hadde spent the king a greate treasour
to make recompense to his highnes if he sholde de herein committ
any thing otherwise than reason wolde / was this day called
before the cownsell and commaunded to bring sureties for the
perfourmance off his sayde promes /

the court

Robert cowley hauing ben long tyme prisoner in the fleete
was this day called before the cownsell and confessing his
default and desiring thereoff pardon was dismissed owt
off prison being bownde first by recognisance as ensueth


pagebreak


The condition off theis recognisance is suche as iff thaboue
bounden Robert do nott repayre withowt license in to the realme
off Jrelande nether sende thither and wright to any man
any matter than being first shewed to twoo off the cownsell
shall be thowght good to be sent forth off then than etc. orells
etc.


Att Otelande the xxijth day off
July being than present as before

A lettere was written to the sheref off the countye off
devonshere to putt in possession one John halleswell
in certeyne landes wythin the parisshes off yalmeton Ermington
as one Richard Stroode hadde iniustly disposse vsurped
hadde nott permitted the sayde halleswell quietly to possesse
decrees / and a lettere allso written on that behalff from
the cownsell / A commaundement was geven to the sayde
sheref to charge the sayde John Stroode to appere before
the cownsell the xvth off october next ensuing
