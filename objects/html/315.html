At westminster the xxiijth day of februarye
being than present as the day before

Vpon a bill of complaynt exhibited by peter vannes
the kinges highnes latten secretarie / a Letter off apperance was
deuised and sende forthe for thomas vaughan on off the sewars
of the chawmber / to repayre to the cowrt wyth all convenient
diligence /


Att westminster the xxiiijth day of february
being than present
The Duke of Norffolk
The duke off suffolk

Therle off Sussex lorde great chawmberlayne
Therle of Hertford

The condition of this recognisance is suche as if thaboueboundne
Edmunde Knevett do appere before the priuye cownsell quindra
pasche next after the date hereof / and gyve his attendance daylye
vnto syche tyme as he shall be by the same dismissed than this
recognisance to stande voyde / or ells etc.

Thomas vaughan reocrded his apparaunce /

Sir thomas hammer knight hauing ben accused off sondry thinges wheroff
soom was no lesse than high treason / bot for that itt both appered thesame
to haue ben committed rather by ignorance than malice / and yett before
the generall pardon / was after a good exhortation discharged
