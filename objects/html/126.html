At Hamptoncourte the vjth day of

The Duke of Suffolk
The gret Chambrelain of England
The Comptroler of household
The vicechambrelain
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretary

The condicion of this recognisance is such that if the aboue
bounden Edward Rawley do his best to apprehende and bring
before the Counsail George Byrd who being suspect of felony
is fled vppon the same and was supposed to haue bene conveyed
by the said Edwarde And if also the said Edward do not
onely appere himself but also bring before the Counsail Robert
Frybody one of his syruantes from tyme to tyme at and at all tymes
within a twelfmoneth next ensuyng vpon Laufull monicion to
be giuen for the same that then etc. orels etc. /
