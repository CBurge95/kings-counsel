present
Tharchebishop off Canterbury
T=The lorde chawncellour off Englonde
Therle off hertford
The bisshop off westminster
Sir John gage
Sir richard riche
Sir John back baker

Letteres wer written vnto the court towching the discoursse
hadd wyth the marchauntes for the payment off the new imposition
off one off the hundreth in flaunders / wyth theyre resolution in
no cace to consent vnto thesame vnlesse the kinges highnes
sholde commande them /

William Barlow clarcke was sent home wyth letteres off
thankes to the commissioners for theyre diligence / to whome
wer deliuered iij frenchemen ij from the marshallsey and
on from the porters lodge to be convayed backe a geyne vnto
the place wher the gowlde was thowght to be hydde for
the better recouerye off the same

Calfhill who hadd long tyme layen in prison in the fleete
was this day after a good exhortation dismissed

A passeport was signed for certeyne spaniardes to passe
by plymowth in to spayne /


Att westminster the ixth day off June
being than present
Tharchebishop off Canterbury
The lorde Chawncellour off Englonde
Therle off hertford
The bisshop off westminster etc as the day before

A lettere was written to my lorde warden with a shedule therein
enclosed / In the whiche shedule was conteyned all suche nombres off
horssemen and fotemen as the kinges hadd appoynted presently

pagebreak


to be sent over by him and others / In the whiche the
lettere the kinges highnes plesir was allso signified vnto him that
he sholde meddle with nowe off his graces seruantes /

Letteres wer sent vnto the court to declare how the cownsell
hadde her proceded towching the allotting of all the sheres
of Englonde to certeyne nombres off menne /


Att westminster the xth day off June
being than present as the day
before

Letteres wer sent vnto the court, specyfying the manner
of theyre discourse here wyth themperowres Ambassadour towching
certeyne poyntes written from the court to be reasoned wyth
him / whereoff the minute remayneth /

Fowr seuerall warrantes wer signed wythe the cownselles handes
for my lorde Cobham / my lord Morleys / Therle off Sussex
and my lorde wentworth / for thallowance off money for the
cotes conduit and eyght dais prest for theyre soldiours

Letteres wer written vnot the court towching the resolution
off the marchauntes aduenturours concerning the payement off
the new imposition etc.
