Att westminster the xjth day off februarie
being than present
The lorde archebisshop off Canterbury
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hartforde lord great chamberlayne
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
The lorde saint John
Sir thomas cheyney treasorour
Sir John gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsses
Sir Antony wingfelde vicechamberlain
Sir thomas wriothesley secretary

Lettres wer written seuerally to the lorde lisle high admirall
off inglonde and To sir fraunces brian viceadmirall to aduertise
them off certeyne skottisshe shipes wythin the hauen off camphire
and to take ordre allso off the xj sayle wy whiche wer in
the north sees they sholde sende vppe to the downes iiij off
the meanest sorte /


Att westminster the xijjjth day off februarye
beyng present as the day before /

The condition off this recognisance is suche as thabouebounden
Jhon and Jhon whereas certeyne lastes off hearing wer stayed
and arrested wythin the porte off douer as gooddes apperteyning
to frenchemen / in cace itt shall hereafter appere thesame to
apperteyne to the sayde Jhon and John and nott to any frenchemen
as was pretended than this recognisance to stande voyde orells
etc.
