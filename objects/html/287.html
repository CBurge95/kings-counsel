Twoo seuerall Letters cer wer deuised the on to sir william Ever
deputie warden off the east marches for anempst skotland, and the other
to sir cuthbert ratclif deputye warden off the myddle marches, to make
secrete enquyrye off all notable attemptates committed by the skottes on eyther off
theyre offices sithen the last treuces taken with skotland, and thereoff to aduertise wyth diligence

The keping off my ladie off norffolk; hows callid horssham was committed to master carrell

after the sayde rate vntill willing the sayde sir brian tuke to sende

made and signed accordinglye

Whereas by the letters of the lode off westmorland and
other aduertisementes / it was signified to the kinges highnes that
the king of skottes was coom to the borderres of skotland Letters
wer sent to therles off westmerland and comberland, to remayne
styll for the sayde pourposes in theyre owne howsis for the saftye
of the borders and discharging them of theyr writt repayre
to the parliament notwithstonding theyre writt receyued to the contrarye

Forasmoche as the kinges highnes was aduertised that oon Monser
the de morvillier, and in his companye oon of the grawnde cownsell
and an herauld off armys wer departid from fraunce by see

wharton and sir cuthbirt ratcliff / by theyre diligent espiall to

Whereas oon carter baylye off westminster exhibited to the cownsell a certayne boke
conteyning certayne confessions off twoo suspectid parsons apprehendyd a lityll before Itt pleased
the sayde cownsell to committ the farther examination of them to the
lord saynt jhon and doctor peter

The lorde saynt Jhon and doctor peter declared to the lordes off
the cownsell what they had fownde in thexamination off the ij sayde
suspectid parsons / shankes and powrdew And perceyuing by thesame
that nott onlye they had committed sondry fellonyes and robberies
butt that there war yet remayning abrode a great neast off
theyre complices partely directid by them and partely knewen other
wise a Letter was deuised to the sayde Carter Baylye off westminster
wyth commawndement and authorite ride vnto suche places
whereas the sayde complices war inhabitantes / and there to take them
forseing that theyre gooddes and cattalles wyth by the assistence off vj
honest neyghbores might be committed to the safe custodye off theyre
frreindes / tyll in suche sorte as they might be forthcomming as iustyce
sholde require

Wheras vpon a pretendyd libertye and priuilege the
wardens of the gowldesmithys had entrid in to a strawngers hows in the
kinges pallaice of westminster aswell serching thesame as carriing
wyth them certayne peces of worcke whiche according to theyre sayde
priuilege they iuged vnlawfull and worthye to be forfett for
that itt was fownde that the sayde strawnger wrowght there in the kinges
for the kinges highnes / and allso for that itt was nott thowght
mete that any officer owght so mallaportlye wythin the
kinges pallaice to serche and vew what the his maieste sholde
haue there a making / they wer callid before the lordes off
the cownsell And there rowndely admonisshed off theyre misbehavour
war warnyd vpon payne of that wolde thereof befall to aduen
ture no more so far
