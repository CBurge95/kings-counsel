Wheras complaint had bene made by the frenche ambassadours
that certain Leade of a marchaunt of Rowen was stayed at
London by the packer for Lack of payment of package money
It was ordered and decreed that Edmunde Harman one of the
kinges barbers / being packer at London for the kinges highnes
shuld shewe vnto the party complaynant that seing the matier
was yet in suspense / and not tryed whither package
wer due for it or no / he was comaunded he was comaunded
to deliure the leade in the meane tyme / vntill the matier
wer determyned and thervpon shuld cause the same leade
to be deliured without taking package money for the same /
And furthermore he was commaunded to absteyne from exacting
or axing any pacakage money for leade of any straungier and
specially of Frenchemen vntill such tyme it wer better examined
whither package might be taken for leade without offence of the
treatyes betwene the prynces or of the kinges proclamacion
Lately made in the libertye and fauour of straungiers touching
the payment of Custumes /
