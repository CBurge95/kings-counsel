Att westminster the ixth day off marche being
than prsent as the day before /

Letters wer deuised and sent forth to the mayour and auldremen
off kingestowne vpon hull / requiring thesame to take suche an ordre
vpon the setting off reasonable pryces vpon all kindes off necessarie
vittayles / as the worckmen sent thither by the kinges highnes might
live pon the wages theyre wages

George browne esquier master off thordonance at callais
presented seuerall bookes to the cownsell conteyning aswell the munition
and artillerie being now wythin the towne off callais as / what
waht necessarie peces wer lacking in the same /

Letters wer sent vndre the stampe to the deputye and
the rest off the kinges cownsell in callais for the setting att
libertie the Ladie Lisle and her dowghters and ministers
and the restoring to the same off theyre apparail and iewelles

A warrant vnder the stampe was directed to sir richard
pollard / to deliuered to sir harry sampson clarcke / to thuse off
the lady lisle aswell for her the payment off her debtes as
for her transporting and other necessaries the summe off oon
hundreth pownde /
