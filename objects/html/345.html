Att hamptoncourt the vth off June
being than present
Therle off Sowthampton lorde priuye seale
Therle off Sussex lorde great chawmberlayne
Therle off hertford
My lorde russell high admirall
The bisshoppe off duresme
The bisshoppe off westminster
Sir Thomas cheyney thresorour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horss
Sir Antonye wyngfelde vicechamberlain
Sir rafe Sadlair Secretary
Master dacres

This day a finall order was taken betwene sir nicholas
poyntz and Jamys higges after the sorte as hereafter
ensuith

First the sayde Sir Nicholas shall make or procure to be
made to the sayd James as good and as sure an estate
in that the ferme that the sayde James now enioyeth / in recom
pense off a nother / whiche he hath therefore surrendred to the
saide Sir Nicholas / as the sayde James had in the sayde
ferme surrendred before the surrendre off the same

Item whereas the saide James hath a parcell off grownde allotted
to the saide ferme whiche he now enioyeth and payeth for thesame
to the sayde Sir nicholas xxs by yere The saide sir nicholas
shall hereafter suffer thesame higges to enioy the sayde parcell off
grownde / wyth like estate as the ferme abouesayde yelding and
paying therefore onelye vjs be yere /

Item the sayde sir nicholas in consideration off the dammages and
travailes off of the sayde Jamys higges shall to him pay and
deliuer wythin xb dais after the date hereoff the summe
of xl marckes Att the receyte whereoff the sayde Jamys
shall deliuer in to the handes off the saide Sir Nicholas the
leasse off the ferme off osylworth whiche the sayde sir nicholas
now enioyeth / by the surrendre off the sayde Jamys / and

pagebreak


likewise all manner off writinges skrippes and skrowles now
remayning in the possession off the sayde Jamys and concerning
the saide ferme of combe / deliured by the handes off the sayde
sir nicholas in tyme past to the sayde Jamys higges / The saide
Sir nicholas at the sayde tyme making or procuring to be
made to the sayde Jamys a leasse as is before sayde off the
ferme whiche he now enioyeth after the manner and forme
of the state he had in osylworth wyth a sufficient assurance
other by recognisance or decre off the cownsell to enioy the sayde
ferme off combe to all pairposes and intentes / as he before
enioyed the ferme of Osylworth / The sayde Jamys likewyse
being bownde for the perfourmance off this finall order taken
betwene him and the saide Sir Nicholas / And a so a finall
agrement to be made betwene them from the begynning off the
worlde tyll this day


Att hamptoncourt the vjth day off
Juine being than present as the
day before /

An ordre was taken that Sir Nicholas poyntz sholde
be discharged off his former recognisance / for all poyntes
sauing the keping off the kinges peace wyth suche as haue
hadde entred informations ageynst him / and perfourmance
off this finall ordre / and his apperance crastino airun /
