Att the moore the xvth day off June
being than present
Therle off Sowthampton lorde priuye seale
Therle off Sussex
my lorde admirall
The bisshop off duresme
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshoppe off westminster
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horss
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretarye
Sir rafe Sadlair Secretary

Letters wer deuised and sentde to my lorde chawncellour
to cawse to be searched all indentures passed betwene the
king or his noble progenitours / and the capitaynes off the
jsles off guernesey and garsey /

Twoo severall letteres wer written to sirgeant hales and to master broke the commons sirgeant off london to appere
att hamptoncourt the xxvth day off this instant month /

A lettere was sent to the warden off the feleete / to send hither
on Jhon weston prisoner /

A lettere was deuised in frenche to the bissopp of constances
vicair generall
