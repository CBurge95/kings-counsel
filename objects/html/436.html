Att westminster the xvijth day off
february being than present
Tharchebisshoppe off Canterbury
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hertford
The bisshopp of westminster
The
Sir Antony browne
Sir thomas cheyney
Sir Antony wingfelde
Sir Thomas wriothesley

Whereas a certeyne nombre off frenche shippes being war stayed at portesmowth
by the kinges highnes commawndement / and one off the same
was declared to be laden wyth hering which if ordre
wer nott therein taken might shortlye coom to nowght /
a lettere and commission was directed to Jhon Milles / Jhon
chatterton / Jhon whight and parre to make incontinently
sale thereoff / and to reserue the money that sholde rise thereoff
vntyll his maiesties plesir sholde be knowen further /

A commission was signed by stampe for Thomas chawmberlayne
to be being sent in the kinges affaires by see / to take vppe men etc
if necessite sholde so require

Antony Draycott being accused off sondrye matters by the
lady draycott his brothers wife appered befoere the cownsell
and forasmuche as thesame hadde for that present no convenient
tyme to hire him thorowghlye / he was bownde by recognisance
to attende as hereafter ensueth

The condition of this recognisance is suche as if thabouebounden
Antonye do geve attendaunce vpon the priuye cownsell from day
to day and nott departe vntyll he shall be by them dismissed
than etc or ells etc
