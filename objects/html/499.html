May being than present
The lorde chawncellour off Englonde
The lorde russell lorde priuye seale

A lettere was sent to master palmer o treasorour off the kinges highnes
crew and fortifications at guisnes to allow clement harleston
hauing the charge off one hundreth horsemen after the rate as
other capitaynes haue ben allowed in like caces there /

Robert gouche marchaunt being bwnde in recognisance
as doth appere before / not to departe owt off the cite off
london wythowt licence and knowlege off the cownsell was
this day for certeyne considerations licensed for vj wekes
to be away / att the terme off whiche vj wekes he was bownde
in vcli and thre fowre suretyes wyth him eche off them in
one hundreth pownde whose names ar morres / wylliam
locke / Christofor litcott and watkins / to retourne to the
cite and to perfourme the contentes off his former recognisance

A lettere was written to the mayour and other the kinges
highnes officers att douour s to suffre no man to passe wythin
vj days next ensuing vnlesse thesame sholde haue a passe
ether signed by the king or by his cownsell

A like lettere was written to my lorde warden
