A lettere was deuised and sent master Stanhop for the
prouision off certeyne greyne ageynst marche next ensuing
and to deliuer suche prest for thexpedition off thesame as
he sholde thinke expedient /

A commission was ordeyned for master stanhoppe to take vppe
carttes for the carriage off the sayde prouision /
