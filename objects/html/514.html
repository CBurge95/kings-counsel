A lettere was written to tharc the bisshop off laidaph declaring
vnto him the ordre taken betwene wylliam bulmer and his
wife / and praying him for thaccomplisshement off the same
to take in to his handes yerely xl marcke to b off thesame
bulmers to be deliuered by his handes vnto the sayde bulmers
wife, and farther to pay vnto her handes all suche summes
as off the sayde landes doth att this present remayne
in his handes

Whereas by commaundement off the kinges highnes a generall
searche hadde ben made thowrowghowt the cite for the staying
off all frenchemens gooddes / and sondry straungers gooddes war
by reason thereoff streyned vpon probable coniecture thesame to haue
ben frenchemens s gooddes / Forasmuche itt appered that diuerse
parcells off the sayde gooddes war clerelye franchemens and other
parcells to be dowghtfull and ambiguows / to thintent that att
the humble suit off the sayde straungers ordre was taken that all
the sayde gooddes shoulde be restored ageyne in to the handes off the
sayde straungers being no frenchemen / and they to be bownde
in recognisance and to fynde sureties that they sholde vpon requisition

pagebreak


deliuer all suche portions as sholde clerely be knowen to be
frenchemens gooddes wnto suche as for the receyte thereoff sholde
be appoynted by the kinges highnes /

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bounden Mariotto / baptista / barthlomeo and benedietto do dewly
and trewly deliuer for the kinges behalff all suche gooddes as being
in theyre possession shall clerely be knowne betwene this and candlemas to apperteyne to
frenchemen / and farther shall abyde suche ordre for that that is
dowghtfull and ambiguous as shall be taken by the cownsell
and shall present theyre parsons att the sayde feast off Candlemas
vnto the cownsell to know theyre farther plesures than etc. or ells
etc.

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebounden
Jacobo and dominion do dewlye and Mariotto etc. do trewlye
obserue theyre bandes expurssed in the conditions off theyre recognisance
and every article off the same than etc. or ells etc.
