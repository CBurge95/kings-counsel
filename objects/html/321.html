Letters wer receyued from Sir thomas Wharton off the date
of the xxvjth of februarie / conteyning sondrye Attemptates committed
by the skottes vpon the west marches / And his meting wyth the
lorde maxwell / and certayne communication had betwene the lorde
maxwell and an englissheman at that tyme /

A Letter off passeport was passid vndre the stampe / for
paskall to departe by the xxvth day off Marche / owt off thys
realm off englond / and marches off the same / wyth xl
crownes and on hors or gelding /

A payre off indentures wer browght in and exhibited to the
cownsell coteyninng the marchauntes off the staples convenantes towching
the paying of the kinges retinew att callais the vjth day off
aprill and the vjth off octobre for asmoche as they sholde shippe
on the on partie / And the kinges highnes license for the shipping
off wolles for oon yere retenyng from the first dau off marche till
the in the xxxiijth yere off his highnes reygne / tyll the last day off
februarye that shall be in the xxxiiijth yere off on the other
partie / whereoff the oon r concerning the staplers indenting
remayneth in the custodie off the lorde priuye seale /
