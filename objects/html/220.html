Wheras John Howburne syruant to syr william Evers knight
Capitain of Berwyke seased as forfait a certain horse of
price of xxty nobles for that redy to haue bene caryed in to Scotland by a
syruant of the Quene Dowagiers of Scotland named
Adam Karkettill A lettere was written to at the
contemplacion of the said Quenes letteres vnto the kinges
Maieste / to syr the sayd syr William Evers not only to
cause the his said syruant to restore the saide horse
vnto the said Carkettill but also to suffre him
to passe by him in to Scotland with the saide horse
Any acte statute proclamacion to the contrary
notwithstandyng /
