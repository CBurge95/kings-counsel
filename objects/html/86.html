Certain letteres wer brought to the Counsaill from the
Treasorer and Comptroller of Calais touching the
declaracion of the matyer put vpp to the Counsaill
by Edmund Smith by waye of Complaynt and touching
a certain plat of the keywork at Guysnes decayed /

Torre which had bene long in exile in fraunce and
after vppon the warrantyze of the Late Erle of Essex
cam in and submitted himself to the king was before the
Counsaill and there declared aswell certain his proceding
in fraunce and Flaunders asalso the handling of him
in Flaundres by Philips and Braunsetre

Certain letteres wer sent from the bishop of Norwiches
Chauncelour and Thomas Godsalue touching the
apprehension of one Thomas Whalpole a sedycyous
fellowe and a setter furth of a naughty booke made
by Philipp Melanchton aginst the kinges actes of christen
Religion /

Twoo warrauntes wer stamped for the payment of
payed to sundry persones

A lettere vndir the Stampe was written to Justice Marvyn
sir william Gyfford knight John kingsmyll and John Waleston
