Letteres wer brought from the Chauncelour of Englande
with the copye of sir John Wythringtons patent in
which he moved that the kinges pleasir might be
knowen whither he shuld make Denyzens after the
course of the chauncerie /

John Heron the bastard brother of Giles Heron was
brought by John Herd one of the porters before
the counsaill and eftsones examyned of his practise
of astronomye and necromancie sithens the prohibition
of the Late erle of Essex and aunswering indirectly
was remitted to warde again and chardged to put
in wrytting all his practise sithens the said prohibition
and the names of theim that haue requyred his
working in it and the names and dwelling places
of all such as he knoweth practisiuers of the said
craftes /
