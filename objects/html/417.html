betwene the day off thentre off the saide and christe
mas next ensuing / this day hanz van fremont
was called before the cow n sell / and forasmoche as
he cowlde nott be browght to suche a reasonable poynt
as the cownsell hadde deuised / the y matter was sett
ageyne att libertie and the sayde recognisance declared
off none effect
