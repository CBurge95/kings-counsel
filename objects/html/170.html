Thomas Marriot of Rugby in the county of Warwik
who was accused to haue spoken certain Lewde and
vnsemely woordes of the kinges Maieste / being examined
before the said Counsaill of the asme and appering
to haue spoken the said woordes rather of a madnes
and frenesy then of any rancour or malice was dimissed
with a lettere to Master Wigston and certain others Justices
of the peax there to cause such gooddes of his as wer
attached vpon his arrest to be restored vnto
him accordingly /
