Att westminster the vth day off Julye
being present as the day before

Simon heynes dean of exeter hauing ben committed vnto
the flete vpon sondry accusations exhibited ageynst him towching
lewde and seditious preaching and the sowing otherwise
off many erroniows opinions was this day called before
the cownsell and after th a good leasson and exhortation
with a declaration of the kinges marcie and goodnes toward
him was dismissed and sett att libertye / being bownde
in recognisance as here ensueth

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebounden
Simon do att all tymes wythin v monthes vpon any convenient
warning attende vpon the cownsell and answer to all suche
thinges as shall there be layed ageynst him that etc. orells
etc.
