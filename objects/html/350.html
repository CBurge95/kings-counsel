Jo weston do dayly gyve attendaunce the pryvye cownsell
attending vpon the kinges prason, vntyll suche tyme as he
be by then dismissed than etc. or ells etc /

chestershire aswell declaring the towardnes off the gentilmen
off the shire toward thesame as d certifying vn the vnd vntoward
nes off the marchauntes off chester /

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if tha
bouebounden whereas Shelton thabouebownden the father off
off thaboue bownden Shelton dicesassed is was indebted
to Nicholas Dikehoff off the hans / and thomas brewar staklin
brewar the no sondrye summes off money as appereth by
certeynyng debenturs remayning in theyre custodye / Iff
this bownden Shelton being executour to the same
departe nott owt off london tyll he be haue fully agreed wyth them
therefore / than this recognisance to stande voyde or ells
etc
