Thomas Walpole being sent vpp by the Chauncelour
of Norwiche and Thomas Godsalue and examyned
of the having of the forsayd epistle did confesse
that he had offended aswell touching the setting
furth of the sayd epistle as alsoo for conspiring
with oon Forde of East Deram touching certain
coniuracions

A lettere was sent to sir Rogier Tounesende
knight and Master Fermour shiriff of Norffolk
tapprehende oon Forde a phisicion dwelling
in east Deram and to sirche his house for
instrumentes of coniuracions and to sende him vpp
and such instrumentes as canne be founde in his
house / vnto the Counsail with convenyent diligence

Thomas Smyth William Graye Richard Grafton
Thomas Walpole and Thomas Cottisford past
wer committed to the Flytt there to remayn during
the kinges pleasir
