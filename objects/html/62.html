At wyndesour the thirde of Nouembre
being present
The gret Chambrelain of Englande
The gret Admiral of Englande
The Bishop of Duresme
The Treasorer of the household
The Comptroler of household
The Master of the horses
The vicechambrelain
Sir Thomas Wriothesley knight Secretary
Sir Rauff Sadleir knight Secretary

John Anthony of the Isle of Tenet in the Countye of Kent / Sir
James pyeres priest / and John Mere of the towne of Cambridge /
in the Countye of Cambridge / kept their apparaunce according
to the tenour of twoo seueral wryttes vndir the kinges highnes priuey
Seale directed vnto theim in that behaulf /

Sir Thomas Champe priest and Thomas Frenche of Cantorburye
kept their apparaunce according to the tenour of a pryuey Seale
sent vnto them in that behaulf /
