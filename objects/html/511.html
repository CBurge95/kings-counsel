Att grenewyche the xxxth day off June
being present as the day of before /

The treatye of peax was concluded betwene the kinges highnes
commissioners and thembassadours off skotlande / and wer signed
and subscribed by thesame in the cownsell chamber /


Att grenewyche First off Julye the
kinges highnes remoued to westminster
