The same day letteres wer receyued from the lorde lisle
with aduertisement off a certeyne exployt doon in skotland
by sir george dowglas / and the order off the convayance off the
kinges hignes letteres to the king off skottes etc.

Letteres wer allso receyued from the cownsell in the marches
off wales towching certeyne lewde wordes spoken by a skott
wyth depositions off certayne parsens towching thesame /

This day cam allso letteres from master pagett
