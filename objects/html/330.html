Certeyne frenchemarchauntes made theyre humble suites to the
cownsell / that whereas by force off tempest a certayne shippe
was driuen to lande and there broken abowght the cost off
Sandewiche / and there arrested as a wracke / for asmoche
as as no man therein miscaried / they might be restored
to theyre gooddes ageyne / Wherevpon a lettere was deuised
to be written to my lorde warden aswell to gyve eare
to the marchauntes / pourchasing the recouerye off they gooddes
pretended to be forfayted vpon a surmysed wrack / as to vse
Att westminster the xviij day off Marche
all ways and meanes to reassonable all parcelles off thesame
as wer conveyed and carried away by any off the kinges
highnes subgettes / To thintent that after dew and mature
consulatation / iustice might be ministred indifferently as sholde
appertayne to iustice /

Letters wer receyued from Edmonde harvell the
kinges Embassadour at Vanise conteyning the conteyning
his request on the behalff off the Venetians towching
his graces licence for the galleis off venise to repayre
to his Maiestes realm according as they haue ben accusto
med /

An information wax was exhibited by the baylifes off yarmowth
towching seditious wordes spoken by oon Rix inhabitant
off the saide tone / whereapon a Lettre was deuised to the sayde
baylifes aswell to sende vpon surely and safely the sayde
Rix as all syche wyt to charge allso suche witnesses as wer
present att the speaking off the sayde worddes to repayre hither
wyth all convenient diligence /

Whereas oon sir william reede clarke for a bull off the bisshop
off romes fownde in his cofir was by the mayour off and
shirifes off norwyche committed to warde a letter was deuised to
the sayde mayour and sherifes / that forasmoche as itt apperid
to the cownsell that the sayde sir william had nott willinglie
offended / nother putt the sayde bull in execution / they sholde
after a good lesson gyven to him to beware hereafter off all
like follies / dismisse gim and sett him ageyne at large /
