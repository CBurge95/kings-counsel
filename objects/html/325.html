Jhon aprice esquier Secretarye to the kinges highnes cownsell
in the marches off wales And Edmunde and charles fox clarckes
off the cownsell and signett to the same appered this day before the
cownsell / and after sondry allegations aswell off the one partie
as off the other for certa aswell to whiche off th for the keping off
the signet sholde appertayn as for certayne duetyes andd dees
incident to the sayde offices for for certayne writinges / And ordre was taken
that forasmoche as itt was nott thowght convenient the writing
and seale to be in oon hande / the sayde Jhon aprice secr
being secretary shold haue hereafter the custodie off the
signett / And all suche fees for the whiche they wer one in debate
and contention to be sequestred in to the handes off the lorde
presidentes hande off the marches off wales and on to be
appoynted at the sayde presidentes discretion for aswell for the gathering off the same / as for
thexcercising off the sayde Edmonde and charles offices off
writing vntill a farther determination sholde be here in
taken / And hereoff Letters to be directed to the sayde
president wyth all convenient diligence /

Certayne men off hampton / declared in the name off the
hole towne / thoccasion off theyre indigence wherebye they wer
nott able to pay the kinges highnes his fefarme as they hadde
ben accustomed / whereapon the cownsell promised to be ernest
ernest suitrers to his maieste on theyre behalff /

Whereas the mayour and commons off the towne off reading had exhibited a certayne
byll off petition to the kinges highnes aswell for thincorporation off the sayde towne
as for sondry other priuileges / the same boke being by the
kinges maiestie sent to the lordes off his cownsell / was by them
pervsed / and thinking thesame allowable / they ordeyned itt to be
deliuered to master dakers and the kinges attorney / they to
make ageyne certificat what they sholde fynde therein towching
the kinges indemnite or otherwise / And in cace they sholde so
thinke good to engrosse itt to be signed /
