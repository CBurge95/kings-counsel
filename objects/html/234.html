Twoo seueral letteres wer written to therles of Westmor
Land and Cumberlande Requiring them notwithstanding
the kinges Maiestyes former determinacion to the
contrary to cause the lord Scrope and all other such
knightes and gentlemen of the County of York as wer
appoynted by the Duke of Norffolk at his being in those parties tattende vpon
them the said Erles to syrve the kinges Maiesty
in those parties to repayre with diligence to Yorke putting
themselfes and their traynes in such good ordre to mete
the kinges Maiesty at such place and in such forme as
his Maiestes President and Counsail in the North
partyes shuld appointe vnto them / and that the said
the said Erles shuld abyde themselfes at sum of their
houses next vnto the borders vntil they knew further
of his Maiestes pleasur in that behaulf
