Vpon an information exhibited ageynst Christofor Assheton
of the countie off barkshere gentleman / by certeyne off the kinges highnes
subgectes towching his misdemeanour among them namely for exacting
vnlawfully in the musters certeyne contributions among them for the
grounding off horsses / and the taking off money off summe off them
for theyre tarriing att home / A commission was sent vnto Sir
wylliam Estsex knight and Alexandre Vmpton Esquier to examyne
diligenteley the hole trewth off the matter and to aduertise thesame wyth
spede /

A lettere was written to Master Stanhop to pike owt iiij or v
off the most substantiall off all the frenchemen remayning prisoners
att hull and to dismisshe dismisse the rest vpon theyre rawnsoms
to be payed in caec we sholde grow to warre /

A lettere was written to the duke off suffolk in the fovem
off James wierstrop repayring in to skotlande by ordre
of certeyne marchauntes off the stylliard for the recouuray off
a shippe taken by the frenchemen and convayed in to skotland
