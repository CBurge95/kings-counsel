A passeporte was signed for edmunde de Atkinson and Michael
de liure to take vppe post horses betwene this and plymmowth /

A lettere was directed to to prouide a convenient
vessell for the transportation off Edmunnde Atkynson in to spayne

The condition of this recognisance is suche as if thabouebounden
Jhon / Jhon / and Jhon do vpon an honest order for the paying
off theyre personall tithes taken wyth theyre curate appere before
the cownsell the first day off the next terme than etc. or ells etc.
