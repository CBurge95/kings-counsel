Att hamptoncourt the xxjth day off
May being present as the day before

Letteres wer sent to Sir John Wallop Sir Edwarde
bray and Sir Thomas poyninges towching the preparations
off themselffes in cace the frenchemen sholde remayne and contynew
in the felde /

A lettere was written to Sir Edward Montacuit etc to examyne
and determyne a matter off contention betwene

A lettere was written to the bishop off bath Sir hugh pallett and
Sir Nicolas fitzJames to examyne on information exhibited
to the cownsell by John Champneys / and to procede to the reformation
off the same /

Letteres wer written to Sir Thomas pope and Sir Edwarde
North to remove certeyne frenchemen owt off the cowntours and
being infected with the plage and to committ thesame to othe
prisons /
