Jhon ramsey having bin off kingston having bin emprisoned
the space of xv days, for certayne seditious and sklawnderows
worddes, for that is apperid to the cownsell that he was dronnke
whan he spake the sayde wordes, they tooke an ordre that the next
markett day he sholde be sett on the pillory, and the cawse
thereof to be sett in writing apon him so that every man
myght reede itt and than soo warnid first that apon his extreme
parill dronken or sobre to vse no more siche wordes sholde eskape
him, for this tyme s to be dis dimissed
