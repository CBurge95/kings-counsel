A letter was receyuid from the kinges of skottes towching the radresse
of certayne murders and brennynges, doon by the skottes in the englisshe
marches

Certayne bookes off guernesey wer redde and ov pervsid

Jhon douglal A certayne sjottisheman t was taken as a vagabonde th by the
vnder marshall and examined by the sayde marsshall confessid that
for a murder he was fledde owt od his cowntrey wherevpon itt
was decreid that he sholde have a passeport and so within xx
days to avoyde the realme
