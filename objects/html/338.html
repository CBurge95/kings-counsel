Att grenewiche the xxijth day off
aprill being than present
Therle off sowthampton lorde priuye seale
Therle off Sussex
Therle off hertford
The lorde admirall
The bisshopp off duresme
The bisshop of winchester
Sir thomas Cheyney thresorour off the howsholde
Sir Jhon gage to comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsse

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebounden
Jamys do appere before the cownsell attending on the kinges
parson crastmo asrasionis next after the date hereoff and
gyve his attendance vpon thesame vntill he by them
shall be dismissed than etc or ells etc

Whereas the cownsell att the suit and request off the
surveyour off callais / had directed theyre former letters
to sir Edward wotton knight thresorour off callais for the
payment off a certayne soom off money layed owt by the
syde surveyour for the making off brike etc for asmoche
as notwythstonding the sayde letters itt appered that pay
ment was nott made accordinglye / This day other letters
sumwhat more ernest wer deuised eftsomes to the sayde
thresorour to dispatche the sayde payment owt off hand

A license was deuised by stampe for the comming over
off Sir Edward bray knight lieutenant off the castell
off callais /

Letters war sent to sir Antony sentleger knight the kinges
deputie in ireland /
