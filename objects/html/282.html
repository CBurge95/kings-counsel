At westminster the xxviiijth day off Nouember
being than present the hole presence of the
day before

A warrant by stampe was directid to sir brian tuke to pay to Jhon
tregonwell the summe of twoo and fortye powndes and xs for so moche layde
owt by the sayde Jhon tregonwell in his iourney to plymmowth wyth the
kinges commission
