A lettere was sent to the court towching the dispatche
from hens for the revocation off garter / wherein was declared
the paines discurse hadde thereoff and for the mari wyth made by thans
off spayne to coom to westminster the next morning / with other aduertisementes

A warrant was sent vnto the master off the ordonaunce
for pikes and other munitions to be sent owt off hande
to guines
