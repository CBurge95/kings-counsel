Att westminster the First day of Aprill
being than present as the day before

Whereas for cawsing off on ferrers on off the pages
off the kinges chawmber being than on off the bugeses off the
parliament / on whight by the order off the common
hows was committed to the towar / this day the sayde
whight was sent for from the sayde towar tappere before
the cownsell / and ther knowledgying his defawght and yett
alleging ignorance in the committing off the same was content
to knowlege to the kinges highnes two hundreth pownde
sterling to be payed etc. The condition whereoff is suche
that if thabouebounden whight for towching a matter
off debt pretended by him ageynst ferreres / do therein
abyde the order off the cownsell (if the same do make an
ende thereoff bewtene this and the ende off michaelmas
terme next ensuing the date hereoff) and in the meane
season nother molest nor troble the sayde ferrers by him
selff or others concernyng the debt as is aboue sayd than
this recognisance to stonde voyde or ells to stande
in full strength / etc.
