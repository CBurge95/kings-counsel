A commission was signed with the stampe to Jhon Kingesmyll
John Miller / and Jhon Whight to take vppe vpon proces reasonable
twoo thowsande quarters off wheat wythin the cowntye off sowthampton
wyltshire / and dorcestshire / and to prest for the convayaunce off the same
Marriners / Millers / shipwrightes / & other necessary labourars wyth cartes he and to take vppe
botes / keles / carttes / & horsses /

A like commission was signed for thomas woodhows for the taking
and prouiding off one thowsande quarters off wheate and iiij m quarters
off Maulte wythin the counties off Norffolk and Suffolk

A like allso was signed for waters and Reps for an m
quarter off wheat and iiij m quarter off maulte to be prouided in the counties
off Norffolk and Suffolk huntingdon / cambrige and lincolbeshire /

A warrant was signed to sir Edward north to deliuer vnto
the handesoff william honning the summe off one hundreth powndes
to be employed as shall be to him appoynted by any thir thre
off the priuye cownsell by thayre warrant for prouision off the
warres

A lettere was writtent to Richard broole capitayne off the kinges
highnes nauye vpon the narrow sees wherein is was declared
vnto him think his maiesties takyng in all parte his demeanour
vpon the see / in the staying indifferentlye suche as are amytie
wyth owr his grace as others / requiring him therefore to
loke better vpon his instructions / and according vnto thesame
to behaue himselff in suche a sorte as itt myght nott appere
that his grace wer in hostilite wyth all the worlde /

A lettere was written to my lorde deputye off Callais
and the cownsell of thesame / towching the ordre off damplipps
execution and a declaration off a his heresies to the people /
wyth commawndement off Butlar / his preist and the
bowtchars wife off Canterbury to be staw arrested / and to be sent
over to by the marsshalls deputye / who had the convayaunce
thither off the said danlippe /

Thomas wiatt and william pikering wer this day
sent for owt of the tower and knowledging theyre offenses
wer after a good leasson and exhortation deliuered and restored
vnto theyre libertye / being first bownde by recognisaunce
in suche fourme as ensueth

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebonden
Thomas and william do appere before the kinges highnes
priuye s cownsell the last day off the next terme / or att any
tyme betwene this and that vpon convenient warning and do be
the meane season off good abearing to the king and his subgectes
than etc. orells etc.

Itt was thowght good if the kinges highnes sholde be so content
that a generall commission sholde be sent in to kent for wyth
certeyne speciall articles and generally all thinges apperteyning
to religyon to be examined / abuses and enormites off
religion off to be examined /

Jtt was allso determined that doctour peter Tragonwell /
doctour oliuer / and doctour bellows or two off them sholde examyne
Cobbe and Sir wylliam off honny lane etc.

Item the bisshops off Ely Sarx Rochestre and westminster
to examyne doctour haynes / and they to haue deliuered vnto
them all the informations / and my lorde off westminster to aduertise
hereoff the rest / and to procede therein with all diligence
