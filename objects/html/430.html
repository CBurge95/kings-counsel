Att westminster the xxviijth day off
Januarye being than present
The duke off Norffolk
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hertfford lord great chawmberlain
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
The lorde saynt John
Sir thomas cheyney treasorour
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsses
Sir Antony wingfelde vicechamberlain
Sir thomas wriothesley secretary

Letteres wer written to the mayour off rye Phillippe
chewte and to fletcher for the spedie off conveyance off
woode over the see for the furniteur off Callais /


Att westminster the xxixth day off
Januarye being present as before

Whereas thomas waters off lyme hadde charge committed
vnto him for the prouision off one thowsande quarter off
wheat and iiijm quarters off mawlte for the furniteur off
his the kinges highnes garrisons in the parties off the north
a commission was deuised and signed wyth the stampe / for the
taking cost vppe off cartes and vesselles for the conveyance
off thesame /


Att westminster the xxxth day off
Januarye being present as the day before

A lettere was sent in frenche signed wyth the stampe vnto
monsir de bies towching the redeliurye off certeyne shippes
stayed by him wythin the porte off boulloyne /
