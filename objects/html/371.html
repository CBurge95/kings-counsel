Att westminster the vjth day off September
being present Tharchebisshop of
Therle off lorde Chawncellour
Therle off Sussex
Therle off herfford etc. as the day before

The condition of thys recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bownden william bowier do gyve his dayly attendance
vpon the priuye cownsell vntyll suche tyme as he shall be
by them dismissed than etc. or ells etc. /

A lettere was sent to richard raundisshe by from my lorde
admirall declaring to him that where as a the captayne of shippe
apperteyning to the great master off spruce had made
humble suit to the kinges highnes to bring in his sayde
shippe in to the temse and there to remayne all this
wnyter next ensuing / his highnes had graunted
him his request on that behalff /
