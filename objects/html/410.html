being than present
Tharchebisshoppe off canterbury
The lorde chawncellour
The duke off suffolcke
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
The bisshoppe off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
The lorde saynt Jhon
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master of the horss
Sir Antony wyngfelde etc.

A lettere was deuised and sent to therle off anguisshe
wherein the kinges highnes pleser was declared towching
his repayre to his maiestie wyth all convenient diligence

A lettere was sent to Sir henry savell and to sir thomas wentworth
and to every off them, commawnding them so to direct theyre
iourney toward london as they myght be wyth the skottisshe
prisoners here vpon tuisday next ensuing the date hereoff
prouided that every s off the sayde skottisshe prisoners sholde

A lettere was written to the lorde president in the
north declaring to him the kinges pleser towching his
repayre to newcastell, and his maiestie license graunted
to him to be absent from the next cession off the parlament
for that purpose /

Letteres wer receyued and redde in the cownsell / from
Sir thomas wharton declaring the hole order off the
late conflict wherein the skottes wer discumfyted and
a great nombre off then taken prisoners /
