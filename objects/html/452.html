Att saynt James the xxiijth day off
Marche being than present
The duke off Norffolk
The lorde russell Lord pryuye seale
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Thomas writhesley secretary

Where as a matter of controversie hathe Longe tyme depended betwene Robert
Throwes Keper of Ludgate and one hanz van Fremont for and corcernyng
the stape of one Paule Rusto comytted to the saide Prison of Ludgate
for certeine debt laied vnto him by the saide hans van Fremont / For
asmuche as in the mature and deliberat debating of the mattier it appered
vnto the counsell that the saide Paule escaped by the necligence and default
of the saide keper / aand ordre was decreed by them on this behalf after the
forme and manner Following / First that in consyderacion of the damages
and expences susteyned by the aside hanz van Fremont in the poursuit of
this processe and following the Courte therefore by the space of two yeres /
The said Robert thrower shall paie and delivered or cause to be paid and deliuered
to the saide hanz van Fremont the summe of Fiftie Marckes / the one halfe
hereof to be paide within xxvjth dayes after the date of this decree / And
thother half to be paide within iij monthes after the first payment /

pagebreak


Remitting neverthelesse the said hanz to all suche advantages of the Lawe
as he can by aney meanes opteine of the saide partie his debtors escaped
in manner afore saide / And For asmuche as the fearre as the saide Paule
might stande in of the saide keper maye be an occasion of his absennce
out of the Realme and so a lett of suche recompence as the saide hanz
might otherwise opteine of the said debt A farther order is taken
that the saide keper shall in no wise mede with the saide Rusto or entre
anye suit against him for any matter to be grownded ether vpon
the payment of the saide Fiftie marckes or of the Firste staye
vntill a full agreament shalbe made betwene the saide Hanz van
Fremont and the saide Paule Rusto for the debt betwene them
where vpon he was emprisoned
