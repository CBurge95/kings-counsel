At the sute of Tho Dauid Vincent of the wardrobe of the kinges
beddes / three seuerall pryvey Seales wer sent for Miles Forest
Robert Browne and to appere before the
Counsail vpon the sight thereof

Richard Ledes was examyned before the Counsail as suspect of
pyracy and standing in the denyal of such matyer as was Layd
to his chardge was committed to warde /
