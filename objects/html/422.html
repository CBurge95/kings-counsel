Whereas richard Cauendisshe comptrollour off the kinges highnes
att douour hauing ben the day the day before in presence off the cownsell
alleged certeyne mattiers ageynst Sir thomas cheyney knight
tresorour off the kinges highnes hows and lorde warden off the
v partes portes / forasmoche as this day appering ageyne before thesame
he cowlde allege allege nothing for the prooffe off his former allegations
after forgevenes asked therefore off the sayde lorde warden the
sayde richard Cauendisshe was dismissed and commawnded
to repayre to his charge

Vpon an information exhibited by one Jerom Michella
marchaunt off venise ageynst the customer and comptrollour off
rye towching theyre vnlawfull demaunde off custom whiche
heretofore had nott ben vsed / a lettere was deuised and sent
to the sayde Customer and Comptrollour, commawndyng them
in cace the sayde information conteyned trewth to redubbe
theyre misdemeanour in that behalff wyth diligence

Whereas William Smith and Thomas Richard Cupper being sent for
to appere before the cownsell the vjth day off this present / war
charged that they in theyre collection off the subsidie off the lordes to hadde
receyued certeyne summes off money off summe off the saide lordes
for thesame / and that not sayde subsidie wythowt declaring off thesame / wherebye
thesayde lordes by reason itt was thowght they had nott yett
payed theyre dueties war in dawngier off summe displeser /
This day the sayde wylliam and thomas appered eftsones / and
being fownde the fawght hereoff to rest chieflye vpon the saide
william smith he was by order off the cownsell commawnded
to the fleete / and the sayde thomas cupper was discharged

Three seuerall commissions wer passed by the stampe
the oone for Thomas broke being appoynted to be capitayne
of the swepetake, to take vppe men soldiours mariners and
vittailles for the same / The other to william horssewell
mayour off plymowth Amadas customer off thesame /
Buller esquier and Barth capitayne off the trinite harry
to take vppe souldiours marryners and vittayles aswell for
the furniture off two shippes off plymowth appoynted to the see
as for the other purposes / The third to Chaw Muffett to
take vpp soldiours mariners and vittailles att bristowe etc.

A lettere was sent to william horssewell, Jhon Amadus
and buller etc. declaring to them the kinges highnes plesur
that off suche marriners and soldiours as they sholde take vppe
by vertue off theyre commission they sholde bestow viijxx in the
trinite harry and one hundreth in george borauenture and
so se both the sayde shippes fully for two monthes from the tyme
and day odd the setting furth off the same to the see . And
furthermore to assigne over and aboue all this xl marriners
to Jhon piers to sent vppe hither by him wyth all diligence
