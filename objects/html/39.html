At Saiinct Albons the iijde of Octobre
being present
The gret Chambrelain of Englande
The Bishopp of Duresme
The Treasorer of householde
The Master of the horses
The Secretarye Master Wriothesley
The Chauncelour of Thaugmentacions

A Lettere was written to sir william Paston knight to certifie
the Counsaill whither he founde amonges Master Lovels
wrytinges or hath otherwyse herd of any release that old
Empson did make to king henry the vijth of Tousetre /

A lettere sent to the Receyvour of the Landes which belonged
to the Late Marques of excetre decessed taduertise the
Counsaill of the true name and clere yerely value /
of a L certain Lordship called Ra Radway or such other
like name in the countye of Somerset and alsoo to
reteyne in his own handes the half yeres rent of the
sayd Lordship now due at Michaelmas vntill he shuld
knowe further of the kinges pleasir in that behaulf /

Letteres wer sent to the Lord priuey Seale to be at the
Courte vpon wednisdaye next

Doctor Hethe Bishopp of Rochestir was sworne to be one
of the kinges counsaill and Joyned in office with Doctor
Thyrleby elect bishopp of Westminster to heere causes determinable
in the white haull /

Sir Thomas Poynynges was depeched towardes Calais to be
Marshall there in the place of Sir Grenefeld knight
and had letteres with him to the Deputye and Counsaill declaring
the kinges determinacion aswell touching the appoyntement of him
to the same office as alsoo of sir Edward Wootton to thoffice
of high Treasorer / And that notwithstanding the sayd Grenefeld
shuld Leave the sayd office the kinges highnes was his good
and gracyous Lord / like as at his cummyng hither to his
presence / (which his highnes woold he shuld accelerate with
convenyent diligence) he shuld perceyve
