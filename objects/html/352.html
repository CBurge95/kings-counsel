The condition of this recorgnisance is suche as whereas the
hadde in thabouebownden Castell had in sondrye placeS
reported oon Cotton grater of the sande countye to haue
spoken certeyne traytorows worddes towching the kinges highnes
and yett appering before the cownsell cowlde averre
no suche thing / if therefore retourning in to all suche
places where he had made any suche reporte / he didde
diclare that he had evill spoken / and the worddes that he
alleged wer nott to be wayed to treason by the iudgement off the
consell / and yett neverthelesse sholde be redye tappere
before the sayde cownsell vpon any convenient warning than
etc. or ells etc. /
