The boke sellars to the nombre off xxvti appering before
the cownsell wer bounde in recognisance off on hundreth
bo pownde the pece to present in writing on wednesday
next ensuing the date hereoff aswell what englisshe bokes they haue
bowght wythin these iij yeres as what bokes allso they haue
solde in grosse wythin the sayde tyme / and what englisshe bokes
off ill matter they haue knowen to haue ben browght in
to the realme and by whome / and farther to stande to all
serche fynes as shall be vnto them appoynted for bying
or selling off the sayde bokes ageynst the proclamation /

Turcke booksellar for deliuering of a certeyne
erroneows booke named Ag Potilla vpon the gospelles
and pistells was committ to be prented was committed
vnto the fleete /

And ordre was taken betwene Ambrose wolley
and Master Audely / that the sayde Ambrose sholde
incontinentlye deliuer vnto the sayde master Audeley x
demye launces ij handpones / And x billes wythowt
any money to be payed therefore by the sayde master Audeley
