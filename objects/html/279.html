At westmester the xxjth day off Nouember
being than present
The Duke of Norffolk
The lorde priuie seale
The lorde grat chawmberlayne
Therle off hertforde
The high admirall off inglonde
The bisshop off winchester
Sir Jhon gage Comptroller
Sir Antony browne Master off the hors
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretary
Sir rafe Sadlair secretarie

Jhon hoddes off Clay in the cowntye off suffolcke having bin sent for, by
a priuie seale, to appere and to bring wyth him certayne money whiche being
in his custody and longyng to on thomas Cowley he had stayed in his
handes to the kinges vse, declarid the manner and the order there off, and
to whome he had deliuerid thesame

A letter was sent to sir william musgraue towching the mastership off
thordenaunce in Carlisle etc.
