Sir Edmunde Knevet knight and Thomas Clere gentleman
wer brought before the said Counsall for that as infourmacion
was gyven they had made an affray togiders in the tenes
play within the kinges highnes courte and in the said
affray had shed bloodde And being examined
of the circumstaunces of the facte forasmuch as they
appered both to be in faute of thoffense they wer both
committed to the porters warde to remayn there
till the mattier wer further examyned accourding
to the quality of thoffence /
