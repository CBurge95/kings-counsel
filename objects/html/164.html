At Dover the xxxjth od Marche
the forsayd Counsail beyng present

A lettere was written


At Cantorbury the furst of aptril
beyng present the forsayd Counsail

wheras antony rous esquier was sent to gusnes to be treasorour of the work
there
A lettere was written to sir John Walloppe knight
to appoynt vnto v hym such a place within the castle of
guysnes as shuld be bothe convenient for the sure assured
keping of such treasour as the sayd rous shuld haue
there of the kinges highnes in his keping as
and also comodious for the payment out of the
same


At Sittingbourne the ijde of
april beyng present the forsayd
Counsail except the Duke of norfolk

A lettere was written to my lorde Chancelour desyring
hym to make out an oyer determiner to the president
of the counsail in the north & the counsail there
ioyning with the same Robert Southwel esquer
etc. whom the kinges highnes sent to those partes
for teh sitting with the saud president for those
purposes

A lettere was written to Clayder baker
Dwelling in saint Maryhill parashe in
London to forbeare his sute which he had
commenced for a gardeyn plat parcel of Salisbury
house against whom the kinges
highnes sent at this present ouer See about his
highnes affayres vntil his returne home again
at which tyme the said baker bringing before his
Maiestes counsail such profes as he had for his
title of the said ground such ordir shuld be
taken in that behaulf as to Justice shuld apperteyn
