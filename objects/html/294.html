Att grenewicje the xxixth off december
being there present
Tharchebisshop off cawnterbury
The duke off Suffolke
The lorde priuye seale
The lorde great chawmberlayne
Therle off hertford
The high admirall off inglond
Sir Jhon gage m comptrollour
Sir Antony browne master off the horses
Sir Antonye wingfelde vichawmberlayne
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretarye
Sir rafe Sadlair secretarye

Whereas oon paskall a strawngier taken late in the night
by the kinges watche was by thalderman off the warde for vnfitting
wordes lewdely spoken to them sayde watche towching the kinges highnes / committed
to the cownter / and from thens, the next day, remouid to newgate /
forasmoche as d the secretarye of venise wyth diuerse other informing
the cownsell that the matter was forged and for malice imagined
ageynst the sayde paskall / required that the parties might be ernestlye
examined . The lordes of the cownsell appoynted for the satisfaction
off theyre requestes Sir Jhon Allen and Sir richard gressham
knightes / to take the examination indifferently off eche partie
and to make relation theroff to the sayde cownsell accordinglye

A supplication was exhibited by the ladie poyntes and redde
to the cownsell theffect whereoff was to declare that siche com
missioners as war appoynted to sitt vpon thenquyrie off certeyne
misdemeanowres off her husbonde Sir Nicholas poyntz war nott
indifferent and therefore sh nother not the witnesses mete to be
admitted to testifye ageynst the sayde sir nicholas And therfore
she humbly required that the sayde wytnesses might efstones
be examined before the kinges most honorable cownsell
and that in the meane season the sayde sir nicholas might
be sett att libertye vpon mayneprise or sureties to appere
from tyme to tyme vntyll the trewth wer tru tried
