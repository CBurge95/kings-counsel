The xxvjth day of Septembre
departed the king and that night
lay att Howlme in a hows somtyme
belonging to Sir Robert Constable
there the cownsell sate nott


The xxvijth day the king cam to lec
kinsfylde


At leckynsfede The xxviijth day the cownsell sate there off september
being present
The duke off Norffolk
The duke off Suffolk
The lorde priuye seale
The great chawmbrelayne of ingland
The great admirall of ingland
The bisshop of Duresme
The thresorer of the howsholde
The comtrollour of the howsholde
The Master of the horsys
The vicechawmbrelayne
Sir Thomas Wriothesley knight Secretary

This day was Jhon Masyn whiche was before appoyntyd to serue in
the place of the frenche secretary, admitted and sworne clarcke
of the pryuye cownsell, during thabsence off Master william pagett

The same day wer examined doctor brainsby and doctor
marshall chapleyns to the bishop of yorcke and doctor dawkin
chapleyn to the bisshop of Bath, who wer the deuisers of tharti
cles proponed and consultid among the charges at pontfrayte in
the commotion tyme

This day wer hard of the cownsell certayne gentilmen of the
borderes complayning of a rade made apon them // by the scottes
and of certayne murdres, robberies and burninges committed by
the sayde scottes

The xxixth day off Septembre at
Leckinsfelde the cownsell of aboue
rehersid than being present

Doctor Marshall, dakins and braynsbye had
certayne articles deliuerd then touching the supremacye etc
and wer commawndid to shew theyre aduise therin
