The baylif and townismen of potson in the cowntye of bedforde
appered before the cownsell for the disobeying the precept of the clarcke
of the markett, wythin the verge, alleging a priuilege grawntyd to the
duchye of lancaster whereof they wer And forasmoche as itt was thowght
that no priuilege might serue agensst the kinges parson, itt was determin
ned for an example that the clarcke of the markett according to
his former precept sholde go agayne to the sayde towne and there
execute his office, and than farther to be ponisshed by order of my
lorde priuye seale, who is chawncellour of the sayde duchie
