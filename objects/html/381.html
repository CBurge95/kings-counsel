Att grenewyche the xxxth day of
september being present
Tharchebisshop off canterbury
The lord Chawncellour off englond
Therle off hertford
The lorde admirall
The bisshopp off winchester
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir thomas wryothesly secretary
Sir rafe Sadlair secretary

Vpon aduertisement that Jhon browne off essex
hadd stayed wythowt occasion a shippe off flawnders
equipped for the warre / and had committed the garde
and custodie of the same to the baylifes off yarmowth
Letters wer sent to the sayde baylifes as well to declare
to the same the kinges highnes taking in evill parte
the saide Jhon browyers procedinges in this behalff / as
to commawnde them immediately to sett at libertye
the sayde vessell / and to vse the master / capitayne and
soldiours off the same wyth suche entretye / as apper
tynedyteyned to his maiestes freendes
