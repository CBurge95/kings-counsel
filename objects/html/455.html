Att saint James the xxvjth day off
Marche being than present
Tharchebisshop off Canterbury
The lorde chawncellour off Englond
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hertford
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
The lord saint Jhon
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsses
Sir Antony wingfelde
Sir thomas wriothesley secretary

Letteres wer written to Master flu flammocke
capitayne off the kinges nauye vpon the narrow
sees for the transportation off Marillac the frenche
Ambassadour vnto callais / and to albde him there and in
none other place

A lettere allso was sent to the lorde Matrevers
deputie off Callais to suffre and permitt Marillac
thambassadour off fraunce to passe by him in to his natif
contrey in cace master paget the kinges highnes Ambassadour
sholde be arriued thither owt off fraunce / otherwise to
deteyne the saude Marillac vntyll the kinges highnes
plesur shodle be farthar knowen /


Att saint James the xxviijth day
off Marche being present as the
day before

Claude dee frencheman requiring to retourne in to
fraunce wyth thambassadour and for that purpose to haue
a passeporte for himselff iij horsses and ij seruantes hadde
for answar that forasmoche as certeyn gentlemen off
Englonde wer stayed att boulleyne he sholde in like sorte
remayne her vntyll the the dispatche off them
