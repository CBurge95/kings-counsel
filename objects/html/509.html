Att grenewyche the xxvth day off Julye
being present as the day before

William Bulmer esquier was dismissed owt off the fleete
and bownde by recognisaunce as hereafter ensueth

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if the sayde wylliam
shall be obedient to suchee ordres as haue ben heretofore made betwene
him and his wife and allow vnto the sayde wyffe xl marckes by yere
att twoo equall portions pr and att twoo vsuiall vsuall termes
the first payment therof to begyne att the feast off saynt Michael
next ensuing / and do agre to all suche summes as allredy
haue ben receyued by ordre off the cownsell and ahue or shall
be deliured by the handes off the clarcke off the cownsell to his
sayde wife than this recognisance to stande voyde or ells to stande
in full strenfth and vertue /

Mason clarcke off the cownsell hauing receyued from the
president in the north the summe off xxvj li att two seuerall
paymentes didde deliuer this day xli thereoff to growen and
reysed off the landes of Johane Bulmer wyfe to wylliam bulmer / didde
this day deliuer by order off the cownsell xli to the sayde wylliam
bulmer and xvjli to the sayde Johane Bulmer his wife /


Att grenewyche the xxvijth day
off June being present as the before

Letteres wer written to to thomas waters and wodhaus
to se suche prouision as they hadde made to be convayed wyth all
diligence

Harddes off Cantorbury hauing ben a certeyne tyme
in duraunce for the making off a seditiows bill was this after
a good exhortation dismissed and sett a geyne att libertye
