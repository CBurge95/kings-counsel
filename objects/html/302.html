The condition off this recognisance is suche that if thabouebownden sir
nicholas do appere before the cownsell attending the kinges maiestes royall parson
cristino mimarker next after the date hereoff And in the meason kepe the
kinges peace / and departe nott owt of the precynct off london passing xl
mile withowt especiall license off the kinges fawknour and all other that
haue receyued wrong att his handes / And as shall be appoynted and de
termined by the sayde cownsell / And betwene this and the date aboue
mentioned present or cawse to appere before the sayde cownsell his ij sayde
seruantes that hurtt the sayde fawkenour / And in all other complayntes made
ageynst him stonde to order and finall determination off the cownsell
And att all tymes during the sayde terme be redy apon a reasonable
warning tappere from tyme to tyme before the sayde cownsell Then
this recognisance to stande voyde / or ells to stande in full strength
and vigeur /

towching his riotows behavour in thassemblie for thelection of the
knightes of kaiermardmshiere / Letters wer deuised to the president
of the cownsell in the marches off the wales / to examin the h matter

A letter was deuised and dispached to sir Jhon walloppe captayne
off the kinges castell off guisnes / with commawndement to him to make
a booke off all the officers and the hole retenew off the sayde castell / aswel
what the kinges highnes allowith to him for everye off thesame / and wher
he payeth to them / as what they be charged to do therfore /
