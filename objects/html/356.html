A lettere was sent to the warden off the flete for to
receyue therle of Surrey prisone to remayne there
prisoner during the kinges pleser, hauing twoo off his
seruantes to attende vpon him / And to suffer now to
resorte to bankett wyth him

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bownden Jhon weston vpon a sufficient warning shall
att any tyme immediately vpon thesame appere before the
cownsell, and abyde suche order / as the sayde cownsell shuld
than determyn than etc. orells etc. /

Att oking the xixth of Julye
being present as before

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bownden Jhon wyllowghbie do from tyme to tyme gyve his
attendance vpon the pryvye cownsell / and nott departe
vntill he be by then dismissed than etc. or ells etc. /

Robert Belson off Aston was this day discharged
wyth a lettere to thomas Carter declaring thorder off the
cownsell / whiche was that the sayde Robert Belson going
to the howse of the sayde carter sholde desire him forgyvenes
in presence off xij honest parsons / And gyve him for his
costes xls / And pay to Jhon Carwyn sirgeant at armys
for his costes xxvjs viijd

A lettere to was sent to the bisshop off exeter for
