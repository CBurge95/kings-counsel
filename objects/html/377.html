ning himselff wyth lewde wordes toward the deane off Canterbury
and sondrye other there was at westminster by order off the
cownsell committed to the marshallsee This day being sent

A lettere was written to Sir robert Sowthwell / Sir
mathew browne / Sir Christofer morres / James skynner
and Jhon skynner / to enquire off a certeyne matter concer
ning the convayance away off the kinges tymber layed to
bristowes charge / and in cace they sholde perceyue
herein more malice than any iust matter off trewth
to ende and determine thesame accordinglye

Whereas a matter off contention had long dependid before
the cownsell towching a bargeyne pretended betwene wylliam
bowyer / and mariotto neretti stra marchawnt strawnger / the
sayde wylliam bowyer being this day called / and apon the finall
hiring off him and suche wytnesses as for his parte he
cowlde bring in / after long and matier deliberation
sentence was gyven therein / in suche sorte as hereafter followeth

Forasmoche as the matier in variance betwen
Mariotto Neretti and Bremont fourmer Marchant
straunger on thon partie And wiliam Bowyer
Alderman of london on thother partie of and vpon
a bargain of wode hathe ben by vs diligently
examyned and that Sir John Gresham / pol
withipol henry saluago and barthilmew Compagno
chosen and deputed by vs by and with the consent
of the said parties to enquyer and examyn the
trewthe of the said MAtier and so to detter determyn
the same by adaye haue signifide vnto vs
by their hande wryting how vpon examynacion
made they fynde suche corruption in the broker
as they esteme the said bargain to be voyde /

pagebreak


According whervnto hathe before vs knowledgyd his
default in that behaulf / We therfor vppon further
examynacion fynding that the partie principall breamont
fourmer declared hym seilf content to sell the said
wode for twelve shillinges the hundrith in redy money
and non otherwise / Doo order and decree that the
said Bowyer shall enioye the saide wode for the
said twelve shillinges the hundrith paying to the
said Bremont redy money for the same Orelles
to be on the libertie of the said Bowyer to
redylyuer vnto the said Bremont asmoche of the
said wode as he shall not be content to take
at the said price By whiche one decree neyther
partie shalbe loser by the false deseipt and
Corruption of the said Broker wiche dothe many
festly appere But ether partie indifferently
to forbere their geyn That is to saye Bower
the gayne he shulde have had in the price
vnder the value and vttering of the chainblettes
that be not worth so muche And the strawnger
suche geyn as he might have had in the vse
of that marchandise to have ben / olde in the meane
tyme And the said Bowyer to performe this
Decree within theisle eight Dais next following
vppon payn of forfacture of his recognisance and
then tappere agayn and further to be ordered
as to vs shalbe thowght convenyent And it
is further ordered that vpon some convenient
daye market daye the broker shalbe
set vpon the pillary and have his eares nayled
vnto the same / to be a dreadfull example
to other for the committing any lyke offences /
