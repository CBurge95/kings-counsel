At northehampton the xxijth of July
beyng present the forsayd Counsail
The

William Sheffeld lately one of the retynew at Calais beyng
apprehended as a vagab by the lord mordant for a suspecious
persone was for that he sayd he had browght letteres in his custody to the lady
Anne of cleves & to the Duke of norfolk was brought before
the counsail and vppon after his examinacion forasmocheas vppon
his examinacion & the sirche of hym there was only fownd certain
Letteres from one Edwatd poynynges of calais to misteres Howard
the old Duces of norfolk woman wief to one of the howardes
To misteres Katheryn Basset and mistres Sympson
women the lady anne of Cleves women which wer but
Letteres of frendship & commendacions he was committed to the
porters ward to be further examined /

beyng present the forsayd Counsail

A licence was grawnted by the kinges Maieste to Roche Irishman one
of the squiers of for the kinges his highnes bodye to convey out of
the Realme in to Irlande the nombre in consideracion that he
shuld buyld a certain fortresse at his owne charge vppon the
borders of the wild Irish to convey out of this Realme into the
land of Irland & to non other place euery yere for the terme of four yeres next ensuing the date
