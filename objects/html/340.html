Att hamptoncourt the xxviijth day
off May being than present
Therle off sowthhampton lorde priuye seal
Therle off hertford
The high admirall of inglond
The bisshop off duresme
The bisshop off winchester
Sir thomas cheyney tresourour
Sir Thomas Antony browne Master off the
hors
Sir Antony wingfelde vichaumberlayne
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretary
Sir raufe Sadlair Secretarye

Sir Nicholas poyntz knight appered before the cownsell
and bo stonding bownde in recognisance as before appereth
aswell to agre wyth all syche parties as be him offended
hath had entred complayntes ageynst him / And farther for
all matters to stonde to the determination off the cownsell /
forasmoche as nether he had endevoured himselff to the
sayde agrement / and yett in the meane season hadd procured
thimprisonment off on Jamys higges / on off that hadde
exhibited ageynst him to the cownsell on information / was
this day committed to the flete /
