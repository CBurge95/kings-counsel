The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bounden Jhon graylye do appere att the next gaoule
deliuerye to be hodlen in sowthwarcke for the countye off

Att westminster the third off february
being present as the day before

Letteres wer written to the mayour and aldermen off
bristow to sende vppe one Austen Larcke prisoner wythin
the s cite off bristow, wyth suche other as he coulde iustlye
declare to be ringleaders off a certeyne companiye whiche
hadde made a tumulte wythin the sayde cite for thimprisonment
off the saude Austen Larcke / to be presented here before
the cownsell /

Doctour chessham hauing long remayned prisoner
in the marshallsee for certeyne wordes Lewdely spoken
by him whiche he cowlde nott well denie was this
day dismissed / bownden furst by recognisance off xl li to appere att
all tymes he sholde be called for wythin on yere /

Sir Jhon baker Chawncellour off the tenthes
Master dacres /

Vpon an information exhibited vnto the cownsell by
on wylliam fylding conteyning that where as he had fur
nisshed xxx men wyth horsses and harnys to serue the
king in his late warres in skotlande / the sayde xxxti by
consent off theyre capitayne named Jhon Asshebye att
the breking vppe off the camp war retired to theyre
countreys wyth theyre sayde hors and harnesses wythowt
restoring any parte thereoff to him / Letteres wer
written to the sayde Jhon Asshebye to se an order to
be taken herein / and to se that immediatelye the sayde
horsses and harnes showlde be war deluired ageyne
to the sayde william fylding accordinglye /

One Christofer Hall off the countye off yorcke appered
before the cownsell / and being charged aswell off his misbehavour
towarde the certeyne powre men off the sayde countye / as off for an
accompt to be made off a certeyne portion off bulmers landes
whereoff he had the collection was commawnded to wayght and
bownde in a recognisance nott to departe wythowt the
cownselles license /

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebownden
Christofor do from day to day geve his attendance vpon
the cownsell and nott departe tyll he shall be by then
dismissed than etc. or ells etc.
