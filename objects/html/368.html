A warrant was directed to Edward Shelbey to pay and
deliuer to robart raymonde appoynted to the captayne off the
castell of ywarcke for his conduit and now wyth him by post
and the conduit of x other soldiurs / his owne wages for one
month and the the wages in like sorte for xij soldioures and
theyre cost cotes the summe off xxvjli sterling
