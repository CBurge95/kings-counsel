Att Westminster the xxviijth day off
Februarye being than present
The lorde Chawncellour
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hertfford lord great chawmberlain
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
Sir Jhon gage
Sir Antony browne
Sir Antony wingfelde
Sir thomas wriothesley
Master dacres

A lettere was written to Richard Cauendisshe to pay
and content owt off hande one John Aster for one last
off hering taking off the same /

Att westminster the

Roger de prat natif off from the contray off graunce and denisen
off this realmme and wyntrop marchaunt off london wer committed
vnto the fleete for collouring certeyne frenchemens gooddes
