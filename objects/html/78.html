It was agreed that a lettere shuld be wrytten to sir Thomas
Wharton signifying vnto him that wheras he had committed
to warde certain certayn of the surname of Forsters and
taken suertyes of sum of them for their apparaunce for that
they wrote to the kinges of Scottes without his knoweledge
desyred to knowe the kinges pleasir for their further
punishement / he shuld procede to the punishement
of theim according to the Lawe of the Marches
