This day apperid before the cownsell robart horsley preist and Jhon
horsley and Andrew sharp husbandmen accusid for certayne contemptuous word
spokyn of the kinges grace , butt both bicause the proofe herof was nott clere, and
allso bycause the case was nott of myche importance, they wer remitted to the
iustices of theyre sheire, both to be farther examined and to receyue ponisshement
accordingly

Letters wer sent to the commissioners of the sewars in kent and sowthsex for
the redubbing of certayne breches fallen in pensey and romney marsshes

Letters wer allso sent towching the same to master chawncellour off thaugmentacions
and master richard pollard
