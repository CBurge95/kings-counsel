Where as oon pasqual a strawngier / being taken late att an vnseasonable
tyme of the night by the watche / for his lewde manour vsid towarde them
and wordes spoken to the derogation off the kinges honnour as was pretendyd
was by the same browght to warde this day he apperid before the
cownsell and there accused off the sayde lewde wordes / and convinced off
the same by ij witnesses / notwithstonding that the wordes war heynows and
deseruid right extreme ponisshement / yett in respect that he was a
strawngier / itt was decreid that he sholde putt himselfes in a redines to
departe the realm in suche sorte that by the first day of februarye he

pagebreak


might be owght off the precinct off all the kinges dominions and
that vnder payne off life / and that in the meane season he sholde
so orde himselff aswell in speache as in dedes / as he might clerly
apper to lyue vndre thobedience off the kinges lawys /

The condition off this recognisaunce is thus / that if thabouebownden
fowke pigott do deliuer in to the handes off the clarcke off the cownsell
all siche bookes or skrowles that he hath in his keping concerning any
kinde off prophesye / and by worde or dede never hereafter medlle
wyth syche fansys / and att all tymes wythin a yere during
the space off oon yere apon all reasonable warninges be redy from
tyme to tyme tattende vpon the cownsell etc than this recognisance
to stonde voyde or ells to stande in full strength and valew
