Item the boti bowchers wife off cauntorbury now being
and att callais / and Sir Butlar clarcke to be sent
from thens hither by the marshalles man who sholde haue
the convayance off dantlippe thither /

Itt was allso this day ordered that a boke off articles
exhibited agetnst doctour haynes sholde be deliuered to the
chawncelllour off the tenthes / master dacres / the deane off tharches
and doctour oliuer / the sayde chawncellour and master dacres to
call vnto them suche othere off the kinges lerned cownsell
as they sholde thinke mete for the waying and considering
of thesame /

A lettere was written to the custumers off hampton and to
Thomas barthew to signifie the maner and the circumstance off the
arrest off a certeyne spanisshe shippe /
