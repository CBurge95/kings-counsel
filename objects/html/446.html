Doctour quent and doctour oliuer who wer appoynted to be
commissioners for the hiring off of certeyne portugalles suspected
of Judaisme browght in this day theyre certificat wherein they
declared the sayd portugalles by dew processe hadd by them on
this behalff to be christen men
