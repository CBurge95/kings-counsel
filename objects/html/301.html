A warrant was passed / to signed wyth the stampe to master richard
pollard / for the deliuerye off certeyne summes off money specified
in a bill annexed to the sayde warrant / to fraunces bassett widow

A Letter was deuised to Jhon androwys / myll, Thomas whight
warham wylliam kingesmill gentilmen for to trye and serche
owt the manner off liuing off certayne suspectid parsons /

A Letter was deuised to thomas tyling etc to apprehende
the summenour off the towne off hadleigh and the f to sende thesame
vppe to the cownsell

A Letter was sent to sir william Eure / conteyning thankes for
his diligence vsed in his office etc.
