A lettere was browght f to the counsail from Monsir
Mareillak the french kinges ambassadour wherin teh sayd ambassadour amonges
other thinges declaring that one Jehan saint Marten a
frencheman factour to a marchant of Fraunce had robbed
his master of vj or vij C crownes and beyng pursued
was apprehended at bristowe and makyng he made request to haue
the sayd thefe browght back hither again a lettere
alledging v C od the sayd that he had lent not past
iiij dayes before the committing of the sayd Robbery v C
of the sayd Crownes to the sayd marchant at the contemplacion
wherof and also in respect of the degree & place of
the sayd ambassadour a s grome of the kinges chamber was
sent in the cumpany of the sayd marchant with a lettere to the Mayor & Sheriffes & bailiffes
of bristow chargyng the same to send vp the grome
the sayd thefe indelaydly with all suche money lies bagges
Jelles & baggages as wer about hym or at the tyme
or that he had at the tyme of his apprehension
