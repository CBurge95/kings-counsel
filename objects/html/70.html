Letteres wer written to Anthony Rowsse Esquyer to repayre to
the courte

Letteres wer written to the depute of Calais to cause all
the retynew of the garrison ordinary & extraordinary
with the Mayer & all others of that sorte to be furnished
with half a yeres vitail before hand / Signifyng vnto
hym that for that purpose the surveyng therof & also for the putting in ordre
of the ordinaunce aswell persones the kinges highnes woold
send shortly ouer thither aswell persones of experience as also
money for the furniture therof / also requyring hym to s appoynt
gunners & to send the nombre of them ouer
