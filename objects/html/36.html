At Ampthill the xxixth of Septembre
being present
The gret Chambrelain of Englande
The gret Admyralll of Englande
The Bishop of Duresme
The Treasorer of household
The Master of the horses
The Secretary master Wryothesley
The Chauncelour of Thaugmentacions

A Commission to Stoner Sergeaunt at Armes
for the consiruacion of the kinges game of hares & partriches
about the kinges Manour of

A Commission to the president of the Counsaill in Wales
The Lord Ferrers sir Nicolas Hare John Pakyngton
Thomas Holt and others / for to examnyn what Juels plate
and ornamentes be embecilled from the Chryme of St dauides
by whom nad to what value / and alsoo what is left
there and to what value the same is / and to put the
same in suretye to the kinges vse /

A warraunt for the payment of money to Master Tuke

Letteres to the Master of the Master of the Rolles & to Boyes
attorney of the duchie to examyn John Lyull of the port
towne of Heythe in the county of Keny accused for
tapping of money and that they shuld certifie what
they shuld fynde in the same
