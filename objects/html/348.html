Att hamptoncourt the xth off Juine
being than present
The duke off Norffolk
Therle off Sowthampton
Therle off Sussex
Therle off hertford
My lorde russell
The bisshoppe off duresme
The bisshop off westminster
Sir thomas cheyney tresourour
Sir Jhon gage Master off the horss Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horss
Sir Antonye wingfelde vicechamberlain
Sir rafe Sadlair / Secretary

Thambassador off lubecke had his answer by the mowth off
my lorde off duresme / and requiring a passeport / was pro
mised that the next day he sholde both haue thesame and letteres
allso off commendation to thestate off lubecke

Whiche was that of suche
debt as was betwene the
king and them they sholde
pay the one halff att
christemas next ensuing
and for redresse to the be made
on the on syde and the others / itt was agreed
that the morrow following allsollern day the kinges
hignes sholde send iij
commissioners to antwarp
there to mete as many
to be sent from Lubecke
