Thomas Cottisford prist chaplayn to the bishop
of Elye and Deryck a flemmyng syruant to
the sayd bisshopp wer brought before the Counsail
by sir Gyles Alyngton knight and Philip Parys
Esquyer accused by oon Thomas Whalpole for the
settyng furth and publyshing of a sedicious epistle
wrytten by Melancton against the acte of the six
articles and vppon their examynacion the sayde
Deryck confessyng that he had the copye of the
sayde epistle of the sayde Cottysford confessing
that the he had the copye of the sayd Epistle of
Blages wief a grocer in chepe in London was
committed to the porters warde vntil the matier
wer further tryed whervppon letteres wer sent

to the Recourdour of London and william Lock mercer
to examyne the sayde Blages wief and to sirche
her house for the sayde epistle and thervppon to sende

A lettere was sent to the Mayour of London to
call vnto him fyve or m six marchauntes of experience
and to enquyre how sugre and Spyces wer sold
at Lizbone and Andwerpe and how the same
might be sold at London and thereof taduertise
the Counsail to thintent ordre might be taken
for the setting prices of certain signe and spyces
brought to London by portugalles in iij sundry
shippes /

A proclamacion vndir the Stampe &
Signet was proclaymed with trumpet that
whosoeuer had or shuld haue by any meanes
any of the money Jueles or gooddes of oone
Albert spoyled & taken awaye of Late from
the sayde Albert at the burnyng of his
Tent / at the courte gate shuld bring &
restore agayn the same before xijth daye
thenne next folowyng vnto the sayde
albert vppon peyn for keping of the same
still in their handes without the sayd restitucion
to be taken for felonnes /

Sir John Fowel of Asheportburton in the county
of Devon prist being sent for by priuey Seale
appered and was commaunded at the sute of
sir Thomas Denys the Quenes Chauncelour
appered and was commaunded to gyve his attendaunce

Thomas Smyth clerk of the Quenes
Counsaill and William Graye somtyme siruant
to the Late Lord Crumwell wer examined
of the causes of their writting Inuectures
oon against an other / and after long
examinacion of them they wer commaunded
to attende vppon the Counsaill the
next mornyng at vij of the clock

Richard Bankes noted to be the Prynter
of the sayd Invectures and examyned thereof
denyed the same and Layed the faulte to
Robert Redman decessed and Richard Grafton

pagebreak


the which Richard Grafton confessing that he had not
onely prynted part of the sayd Invectures but alsoo had
in his keping a certain sedicious epistle in thenglishe
tongue / writen by Melancton contrary to thacte
of vj articles for christen religion was committed to
the porters warde /

The wief of Blage a grocer in Chepe
appered and was examyned touching the deliuery
of a sedicious epistle of Melanctons vnto Thomas
Cottisford prist and confessyng aswell the manner
of the deliuery of the sayd epistle to the sayde
prist as alsoo declaring that she had it of oon
Richard Grafton a prynter was dimissed

Deryck being eftsones examnyed of the forsaid
epistle and being founde as farre as the counsail
could perceyve not gretly culpable was dimissed


pagebreak


Thomas Walpole being sent vpp by the Chauncelour
of Norwiche and Thomas Godsalue and examyned
of the having of the forsayd epistle did confesse
that he had offended aswell touching the setting
furth of the sayd epistle as alsoo for conspiring
with oon Forde of East Deram touching certain
coniuracions

A lettere was sent to sir Rogier Tounesende
knight and Master Fermour shiriff of Norffolk
tapprehende oon Forde a phisicion dwelling
in east Deram and to sirche his house for
instrumentes of coniuracions and to sende him vpp
and such instrumentes as canne be founde in his
house / vnto the Counsail with convenyent diligence
