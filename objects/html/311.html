The condicion of this recognisance is suche / that if thaboue
bownden Edmunde and charles do during the parlament attende
and ons in the weke at the Least do present themselffes
before summe of the cownsell wherebye the cownsell may be
certified of theyre attendaunce / and after the parlament
do wayght from tyme to tyme / and do nott departe
wythowt license off the cownsell then this recognisance
to stande in none effect / or elles to stande in full
strength and vigeur /
