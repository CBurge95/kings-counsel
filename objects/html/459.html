being than present as the day before /

John Glouer of barking fissherman for disobeying off a
proclamation made in the kinges highnes name was this day committed
to the countour there to remayne tyll satterday next ensuing and
than to be sett att barking vpon the pillorye /

Sir John Clere Stafford esquier / Thomas
Clere and husey wer committed to the fleete for
eating off flesshe vpon good friday

Arondell whiche was committed to the fleete for keping
a borde off flesshe thowroghowt the lent wythin his hows was
this day deliuerde / bounde first by recognisance as hereafter
ensueth

The condition off this recognisane is suche as if thaboue
bounden Arondell do first repayre vnto the mayour and recordour
off london humblye asking then forgiuenesse for certeyne lewde
wordes spoken off them att his being wythin the countour and
appere from tyme to tyme before geve his dayly attendance
vpon the counsell vntyll he shall be by then dismissed
than etc. or ells etc.

Pedro de Baguerras espaniard hauing ben committed
to the marshallsee taken as a pirate among a nombre
off frencheman at thyle off wyght was this day vpon
sureties bounde for him by recognisance as here ensueth
dismissed and sett att libertye /

The condition off this recognisance as suche as if thaboue
bownden diego Antonio and lopes do present the saide
pedro baguares to the counsell att the ende off thre monthes
next ensuing the date hereoff / and att all other tymes allso
as the sayde pedro shall be called for betwene this and that
than etc. or ells etc.

The condition off this recognisance is suche as whereas
it hath pleased the kinges maieste to license the saide Jhon
burgh to go to the sees at his owne aduenture for rapfordes
to be taken off the frenchemen / Iff the said Jhon burgh
do nott in any wise take or spoyle any off themporowres
subgectes not any other being in amitie with his maiestie
butt onely take his aduantage off frenchemen by way
off reprisall and of skottes as ennemies / And allso do
make a certificat to the counsell vndre his hande writing
before his setting owte what nombre off shippes and other
veasselles he woll take wyth him which the names and burden
for thesame and do wythdraw himselff from the sees
when he shall haue knowlege by proclamation or otherwise
that the kinges maiestie shall haue aduicholed this licence and
revoked the said Jhon burgh than etc. or ells etc. specially
or generallye among others wythowt attempting any thing
after thesame shall coom to his knowlege than etc. or ells etc.
