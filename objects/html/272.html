The first day of Nouember At hamtoncourt
than being present
The lorde Chawncellour
Tharchebisshop of cantorbury
Therle off hampton
The lorde great chawmberlayne
Therle off hartford
The lorde admirall
The bisshop of winchester
Sir Jhon gage comptroller
Sir Antony browne master of the hors
Sir Antony wingfelde vichawmberlayne
Sir thomas writhesley secretary
Sir rafe Sadlair secretarye

A generall rapetition was made by the lorde priuye seale
off all siche thinges of importance as had bin passid among the
cownsell attending apon the kinges parson during the progresse
and, in what estate termes all other thinges dyd at this present stonde
aswell owtward matters, as matters of the realm

The state of the cas of quarendon betwene the kinges grace and
Sir Antony lee knight was declarid by the lorde chawncellour

Ans information was deliuered to the cownsell by on Jhon lisse off
leystofte in the cowntye off suffolk ageynst thomas bocking the yonger and
the and Jhon barthilmew the father and Jhon barthilmew the soon
and of Salthows in the cowntye of Northfolk And Cowley
of t of leystoft in the cowntye of for the convayance of certayne
corne owt of the realm contrary to the kinges proclamation whereapon
it was decreid that certayne priuiseales sholde be made for the calling
vppe of the parsons so accusid by the sayde lisse

A letter was sent from themperowres embassadoures, desiring in
the favour of certayne flemminges, ageynst whome was made proces in
thexcheker for reteyning strawngers in theyre howsis ageynst the
statute And therewith was deliuered allso a supplication of
the same flemminges to the kinges highnes


pagebreak


A letter was receyuid from the kinges of skottes towching the radresse
of certayne murders and brennynges, doon by the skottes in the englisshe
marches

Certayne bookes off guernesey wer redde and ov pervsid

Jhon douglal A certayne sjottisheman t was taken as a vagabonde th by the
vnder marshall and examined by the sayde marsshall confessid that
for a murder he was fledde owt od his cowntrey wherevpon itt
was decreid that he sholde have a passeport and so within xx
days to avoyde the realme


The second day of Nouember
At hamtoncourt than being present
The hole presence of the day before

Strayghtley the elder having bin sent for by a Letter
from the cownsell made his apperance

A decre was made to stay an information putt in to the
excheker ageynst certayne flemminges for reteyning of strawngers etc

iiij sondrye priuye seale wer sent owt for the calling vp apperaunce of sondry parson
to make answer to the complaynt and information ageynst them for con
vayance off corne etc On for barthilmew Jhon barthilmew the
father and Jhon barthilmew the son ioyntly, of Salthows in the
cowntye off Northfolk On for Thomas bocking of clay in the cowntye of northfolk on for thomas cowley
off leistoft burston in the cowntye of Suffolk and the iiijth for Hoddes
hoddes of leistoft in the cowntye of suffolk
