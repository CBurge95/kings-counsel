A lettere was written to the sherif off oxon and barckshire
to sende vppe twoo parsons occused by christofer Assheton
off certeyne theyre lewde demeanoure att the musters /

A lettere was sent to Christofor Assheton to repayre hither
and to allege all suche prooffe as he cowlde dewlye iustifie
towching two parsons by him accused /

Beddle Myddleton / Lant / Kele / and maylour
browght in this day theyre certificat in writing according
to theyre recognisance taken off them the xxijth day off April

A lettere was sent to the mayour off Tanbye for the staying
off certeyne shippes off Crosewyke be

A passeporte was signed for the passage off my lorde my lorde parre
for post horsses by they way in to the north
