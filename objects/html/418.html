Whereas one royde off the cowntye off noff norffolk
gentilman hadde made apon certeyne conditions to
wythipowle a playne sale off all his gooddes and landes
forasmoche as itt appered to the cownsell , the sayde bargeyne
to haue ben made onely to defraude his creditoures, itt
was declared thesame to be off none effect / and the
sayde creditoures to be att libertie to sew theyre debt as
the sayde bargeyne notwithstonding

Therles off Casseilles and off glencorne the lorde maxwell
wyth all the other skottisshe prisoners toke theyre leaues
off the cownsell to departe to london and so to go from thens homewarde
the monday after

Letteres wer written to sir thomas wharton to declare vnto
him the ordre off the skottisshe prisoners departyng and
theyre appoyntement to be att carleisle on tuisday the
tenth off Januarie / to whome wer allso sent a packett
off the sayde skottisshe prisoners to be conveyed in to skotlande
for to theyre freendes there, towching theyre pledges
to be sent to carleil the day abouenamed /

A passeport was sygned by the cownsell for carleil
the poursuivant at armes to prouide for the sayde skottisshe
prisoners horsses and syche other necessaries as they sholde
made by the way
