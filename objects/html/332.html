Att westminster the xxjth day off Marche
the cownsell sate nott for that they sate bothe
fornoone and afternoone at the parliament


Att Westminster the xxijth day off Marche
being than present as before

Ordre was gyven to the bisshop off winchester to dismisse
tavernowres brother / who had ben long tyme in the sayde bisshops
hows as prisoner /

A Letter was written to fawke griuell and Andrew flammocke
for thenquire of certayne parsones that wer accused for inordinate
hunting in the kinges harrares / to the greate hinderance off the race


Att westminster the xxijth day off Marche
being than present as before

Whereas oon wylliam Reede clarcke for a bull off rapaire fownde
in his vse was by the mayour and sherifes off norwyche committed
to safe custodye / forrasmoche as itt appered to the sam cownsell
herin to be no malice butt follye rather and negligence / A
Letter was deuised to the sayde mayour and adlerme sherifes
to sett the sayde wylliam reede att large /
