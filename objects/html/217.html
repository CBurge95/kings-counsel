Morgan the kinges goldsmith was requyred by a lettere
from the Counsaill to provide certain plate of the
best and slightest facion / to the value of C markes
and to deliure the same to master Morisyn to be gyven
for the kinges rewarde / to Sainslans of Astorogh w a
gentleman of Pole who cam hither to see the king & the
Realme /

A lettere was sent to the Deputy Treasorer &
Comptroller of Calais / signifyng vnto hym the
kinges highnes pleasyr that in cace Duvelyn bulwerk
wer wrought vp so high that it was defensible
like as the kinges highnes had bene aduertised both
by the present Reaport of Rogiers here as by letteres
fromthens they shuld see the saved that was wrought
alredy vntill the next yere and send all the
working & Labourars at it to Guisnes to be
employed there by their discrecion that had the
charge of the workes there /

Richard Taylour prist parson of feltwell in Norfolk
being sent for tappere before the Counsail vpon
in accusacion that he did fauour the bishop of
Rome / was brought before the Counsail by Hopkyn
the purswyvant & vppon hy examinacion the matter
obiected against appering to be vntrew he was dimissed /
