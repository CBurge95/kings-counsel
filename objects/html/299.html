Att westminster the xviijth day off Januarie
being than present
The lorde chawncellour
The duke off Norffolk
The duke off suffolk
The lorde priuye seale
The lorde great chawmberlayne
Therle off hertford
The bisshop off duresme
The bisshop off winchester
Sir thomas cheyney tresorour
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horss
Sir Antony wyngfelde vichawmberlayne
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretary
Sir rafe Sadlair secretary
Sir richard riche chawncellour off thaugmentacions
Sir Jhon baker chawncellour off the tenthis

Assheton off London appering by order off the cownsell before
Sir thomas writhesley secretarye knowlegid before the same the debt
off on hundred pownde to be pai the kinges highnes / to be payed etc

The condition of this recognisance is suche that if thabouebownden
assheton gyve his attendaunce so vpon the lordes off the cownsell during
the space off oon yere as he may att all tymes apon a reasonable warning
appere before thesame than this recognisance to stande voyde orells to stonde
in full strength and valleur


Att westminster the xx xixth day off Januarye
being than present as the day before

The condition off this recognisance is this that if thabouebownden
hugh dingley betwene this and quindena pasthe be redy att all
tymes to appere vpon a reasonable warning, before the cownsell
attending vpon the kinges most royall patson than this recognisaunce
to stonde voyde / or ells to stande in full strength and valler
