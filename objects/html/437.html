hall do within on monethe after the date hereof preasent him Sealf
before the Lorde president of the kinges highnes cownseill in the northe
and rendre vnto him a Just accommpt of the receytes of william Bulmers Landes
where of he hath hadde the collection / and paie vnto suche as the said Lorde
preasident shall apoynt therefor eall suche arrerages, as shalbe dewe
ether of the saide collection ether ells of suche parcell of the Lande as
he him sealf hath the ferme of / and delyuer vnto the saide Lord presydent
all suche rentalles and other thinges which he hathe in his custody or other
wyse can com by apperteynig to the said collection, and from hensforthe
