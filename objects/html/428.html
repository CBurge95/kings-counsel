Att westminster the xxviijth day off
January being than present as the
day before

Letteres wer written to the commissioners off Sewars in
the countye off Southsex for the spedye repayring off the
breache in pevensey marsshe /

Letteres wer written to the lorde lieutenante in the north
parties to vse his owne discretion for the settling off his
abode ether in Aliuewike or ells in newcastell Forseing
that in cace he sholde conclude to continew att newcastell he
sholde nott loke for suche letteres as sholde be sent hither from
Carleil to be sent first vnto him / for that the compasse
was to great

The kepar off ludgate submitting himselff to the ordre
off the cownsell was dismissed from the flete

The condition off this recognisance is suche as iff thabouebounden
Throwar do stonde by all suche order as shallbe taken by the
cownsell betwene him and hanz f van fremont betwene this and
ester next ensuing tha netc. or ells etc.
