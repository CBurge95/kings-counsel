Sir robart welle curate off collchurche accusid by two seuerall
witnesses / for the wreathing of the viijth chapter of daniell, to his
owne imagination / and expownding thesame as written off the kinges
parson, lewdlye and traytourowslye, therin deprauing his maiestes
godlye procedinges, dewlye and obidentlye convinced theroff was
committed to the towar
