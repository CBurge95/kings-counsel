Thre seuerall letteres wer sent to sir Hugh Pollard
syr Hugh Paulet knightes and william Portman syrgeant
at the Lawe to repayre to Plymmouth there to wyne
with Master Trigonel in Commission for thexaminacion of an vnlawful
assemble / made there by thinhabitantes vppon a portugalles ship
and to doo further as the said Master Trigonell shuld aduertise
them in the kinges behaulf

Twoo seueral letteres wer written oon to syr John Fulforde
knight Shirif of Devon tattende vppon the saide Commissioners
and to doo as they shuld appointe him on the kinges behaulf
touching thadministracion of Justice for the said assemble / and
thother to Richard Edgecombe Richard Turbervyld Lewes
Fortescue / Esquyers / and John Thomas Mayer of Plymmouth
to giue enformacion to the said Commissioners and further
tende as they shuld assigne theim /

Twoo letteres of apparance wer sent the oon to Erneley
and thother to Rangingland

A lettere was sent to syr Thomas Neuill knight wherin was
signified vnto him the submission of William Pett prayng him
that he would remitte his displeasyr against the said pett / and
to take him again in to his fauoure /
