A lettere of Justice was written to syr John Villers knight
George Vincent and Robert Catelyn gentleman

Wheras it appered by to the Counsail by a lettere sent vnto the lord priuey Seale
then from George Vincent and Robert Catelyn that Christopher
Fowler parson of Stony Staunton shuld haue spoken thesame to and sayde
often tymes to one Beamount thise wordes that he the sayd parson Fowler knewe one or two parsones which
did beare vnto the kinges highnes no good hart A lettere was written vnto
syr John Villers knight and the sayd George Vincent and Robert
Catelyn to call before them the sayde Beamount and texamyn him
in secret wise what proves and witnesses he had that the said
Fowler hathd made the sayde reaport vnto him and when he made the same first vnto hym And in cace he
could bring furth any witnesses to sende all to southen incontynently and to
examyn them what they knew in the premisses Requyring them
taduertise the Counsail what they shall fynde therin with convenient
diligence accordingly
