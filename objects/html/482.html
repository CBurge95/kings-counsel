A lettere was written to the duke off Suffocke to cawse the
copies off Sir george lawsons bookes to be deliuerd vnto the
executours /

A nother was thesame day directed allso to the duke off suffolk
declaring thappoyntement off the treasorourship off barwyke to
Sir william Malorye / in cace he sholde accept thesame /
