The compaye companye off brewars wer before
the cownsell / and wer commawnded to appoynt vj habel
men and expert in brewing to be sent to barwyke
thesame to be presented on monday next before the
cownsell / in suche ordre as if nede sholde be they
might departe on tewesday /

The company off Coopers wer commawnded to bring in
writing vpon monday next what nombre off barrelles
and constrelles they wer hable to furnisshe wythin
vj wekes / and to appoynt allso vj hable men off
theyre occupation to be sent to barwyke /

The companye off bakers wer like wyse commaunded
to appoynt vj hable and expert men off theyre
occupation to be presented before the cownsell on
monday next / thesame to be sent to barwyke
