The condition off this recognisance is suche as iff
thabouebounden gyles and Euan do att any tyme
that they shall be called make answer ether in thexcheker
or before any twoo off the kinges cownsell towching a
certeyne quantite off bere seased by one baldewyn
smyth marchawnt haberdasshar off london , and abyde
suche order therein as shall be taken by thesame than
etc. orelles etc.

Three seuerall letteres wer written, to the viscount lisle
lorde warden off the marches, Sir thomas wharton and
therle off Anguisshe / to vse all the best meanes they myght
to sende cawse wyth all diligence a plott to be made off skotlande
and to send the same to the kinges highnes
