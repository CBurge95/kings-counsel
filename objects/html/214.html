Capitayn of Carlisle for his evill vsing of the king es
working there it was ordeyned that the said bishop
syr Thomas Wharton and Edward Egenby shuld examyn
the same and therupon aduertise the king es highnes
and for those purpos es a l ette re was sent to the said bishop
syr Thomas Wharton and Edwarde Egleby / and
an oth er l ette re to the saide capitain / to vse himself more
temp er atly /
