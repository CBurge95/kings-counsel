Wheras Robert Robynson and others of the towne of the towne
of Maydeston in Kent / put vp exhibited vnto the kynges Maieste a bill of complaint against doctour
Leffe Master of the colleage there and and oon walter herenden /
it was agreed that a lettere shuld be directed vnto syr Thomas
Nevill Sir Thomas Willoughby knightes Thomas Moyle
and Walter Henley Esquyers / to reapyre therhe furthwith vnto the
sayd Towne to examyne the said matyer and of
their procedinges therin to aduertise the sayd Counsaill
in writing vndir their Seales the furst Daye of
this next terme in the sterre chambre at westminster

A lettere was sent to the Chauncelour of the Augmentacions
aunswering to his lettere sent vnto the Counsail and requyring
him to set at libertye such of Pates syruantes as he before and
Master Pollard had before examyned /
