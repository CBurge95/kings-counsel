being present /

The gret ChamberLain of Englande
The Erle of Hertforde
The gret Admirall of Englande
The Vicechamberlain
Sir Rauf Sadleir Secretary
The Chauncelour of Thaugmentacions
The Chauncelour of the tenthes

A lettere vndir the Stampe was addressed vnto therle
of Westmerlande Requiring him in cace he shuld haue any certain
knoweledge that the Scottes went about to attempte any entreprise
against Berwycke or any other of the border / he shuld repayre
to Berwyke and there make his abode for the defence of the
same /

of June / the Counsail did not sitt
