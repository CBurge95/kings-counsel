A commission was directed to master worsly capitayne off thisle
off wight / to master milles / Jhon whight and of Sowthwyke and
master chatterton to take a trew inventarye off all the gooddes wythin certeyne
frencheshippes stayed att portesmowth / wythowt permitting any parte
thereoff to be pourloygned or embesealed /

Letteres wer sent to Sir Jhon Walloppe / for the spedye providing
of vittaile in to the castell off guisnes / and farther to haue a good
eye vnto the pale etc. /

Two seuerall letteres wer directed the one to Sir Edward wotton
and a nother to Master palmer to pay vnto Callais and Guisnes
the kinges highnes poursuiuantes for theyre iournays and viages to
be to them accustomed committed for his highnes affaires / according
to the rate in suche caces heretofore accustomed /

A lettere was written to Richard Cauendisshe to agre with a Jhon
Aster for a last off hearing iniustlye taken from thesame att
dovour
