Whereas thomas barnabie att the suit of Roche the alderman
was arrested and committed to the cownter / Forasmoche as the same
thomas had a Letter signed with the cownselles hande wherein by virtue whereof defense
was made that aldermen to no man sholde ancombre him during / the tyme he sholde
be occupied in the kinges affaries / he was by ordre off the cownsell
dismissed owt of prison / and the sayde Roche commawnded
to pay his fees / and other expensis during the tyme off his emprisonnement


Att westminster the xvth day of february being
than present as the day before

Wheras Edmonde fox and charles fox on and on calffhill
had exhibited to the cownsell a booke conteyning sondry heynows
articles ageynst the presydent of the cownsell off the marches off
walis / This day the sayde president was commawnded tappere and
to make answer thervnto / And forasmoche as by his answeres
itt appered to the cownsell / that the accusations rather holy proceded
off mallice than off any good z seale or desire of any
reformation and onely nothing being allegid for the iustifying
of the same butt surmises coniectures and allegations off
reportes / for that it was thowght an evill example that
any man sholde haue libertye vpon no grownde so malitiowsly
to accuse th any high minister off the kinges iustice / for
thexample of other / the sayde accusers all three wer committed
to the flete / butt forasmoche as the sayde edmonde
and charles fox allegid themselffes to be burgeysyl off teh
parlament / and claymed the priuilege off the hows / the
cownsell admitting herein theyre iust request dismissed
them from the sentence off imprisonement / for that tyme /
and bownde the eche off them a in a severall recognisaunce
off ij li / tappere before the cownsell ons in the weke during the parlament / and
after the parlament tappere from tyme to tyme / till they sholde
be licensed to departe / And the sayde Calffhill in the meane
season according to the first resolution / was sent to the fleete
