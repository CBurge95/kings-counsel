Att westminster the viijth off September
being present as the day before /

A commission was deuised by stampe to duffibe and
giles harrison for the raking vppe off caske /

Two severall placarttes wer assigned to take vppe horsses
carttes and all other carriages / the on for sir william prinson knight
and Jhon parker esquier / and a nother for thomas hungat
and william brakabery for the convayance off certeyne stuff northward

A passe placart was signed by the cownsell in the behalff
off Master James lyrmowth master off howsholde to the king off skottes
and his embassadour her / aswell for the taking vp off carttes
and carriages for the conveyaunce off his stuffe to yorcke as
for his good entreteynement by the way /

A commission was directed to the bisshop off chichester
The mayour off chichester master knight and master whight
off Sowthwyke / for the triall owt off an author off a
certayne seditious bill fownde in the open felde beside
chichester

Lettesr wer written to the duke off suffolcke with the decla
ration off the kinges his highnes plesor towching the levying off
ouen and preparation of his own parson northward

The kinges ropers hauing had a commission for the taking
vppe off clapborde / for that the pourpes for the whych they had
the sayde commission was ended / this day they surrendred
thesame /


Att westminster the xjth off september
being than present / as before /


Att westminster the xijth off September
being than present
The lorde chawncellour off englond
Therle off hertforde

Sir rafe Sadlair

Whereas wylliam bowyer marchaunt and aldreman
of london vpon a contention betwene on Mareotti
and him determinable by the cownsell, was bownde by recognisance
of one Ml to gyve his daylye attendance vpon the sayde
cownsell, forasmoche as the kinges highnes this day remoued
he was called, and required to make summe convenient ende
wyth the sayde marchaunt betwene this and monday next ensuing
or ells to retourne to his former attendaunce / And for that
tyme he was licensed to be absent /
