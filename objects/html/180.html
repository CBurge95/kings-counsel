the Reign of king henry theight &
the xxijth day of Aprill
being present /

The Chauncelour of Thaugmentacions

It was agreed by the counsail that foasmochas it appered
aswell by the answer of Master Merven the ius Judge to the a lettere
sent vnto hym & John Gunter from the Counsail the xvth of this
present moneth asalso by sundry other vehement presumptious that sir Robert
sandwych prist & had accused the syr John mychael prist parson of Racton
vppon malice by meanes of syr geoffray poole knight proceding only
of malice that the sayd parson who was in the tower shuld be that vppon
that accusacion shuld be let to bayle


At Grenewhich the xxiij day of
Aprill being St Georges day
the Counsaill did not sit
