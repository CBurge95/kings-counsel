Att Hamptoncourt the xxviijth day off
May being than present
The lorde russell lord privy seale
Therle off hertfford
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
Sir Antony browne
Sir thomas wriothesley
Master pagett /

Whereas a lettere was sent to Sir Edward north and
Sir Thomas pope for the settyng att loge large off certeyne
frenchemen lying in sondry prisons in london / and yett
neverthelesse by letteres off the frenche Ambassadour itt appered that
one Estienne de val remayned in the marchallsey / letteres
wer eftsomes directed to the sayde Sir Thomas and Sir Edward
to dismisse him in like sorte as they had dismissed the rest

A lettere was sent to Sir Christofor Morres to deliuer to Nicolas
gainsforth deputye to the lord gray of wylton captayne off the
Castell off Hampnes for the furniture off the sayde Castell halff
a last off Corne powder / halff a last off serpentyne powdre
c pikes C bowed and c shefe off Arrowes /

A lettere was sent to the mayour etc. off wykham to aapprehende
one laurence who had accused off treason the curate off the sayde
towne / and to sende hither thesame in safetye /
