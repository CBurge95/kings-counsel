being than present
Tharchebisshop off Canterbury
The lord chawncellour
Therle off hertford
The highe admirall off inglond
The bisshoppe off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony wingfelde vicechamberlain
Sir thomas wriothesley secretary
Sir rafe Sadlair secretary

Letters wer written to the mayour and Customers
off bristow declaring the kinges highnes pleaser towching
theyre viage in to spayne / whiche his grace licensed
them to follow notwithstonding a former restreynt
generally made to all portes and creakes off inglonde
that were no veassell sholde make any viage to the
other syde off the see tyll his graces pleser sholde father
be knowen on that behalff

Lyke letters wer allso written to the mayour &
customers etc. off Sowthampton
