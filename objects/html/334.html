Att westminster the xxviijth day off
Marche being than present
The duke off Norffolk
The duke off suffolk
Therle off Sowthampton
Therle off Sussex
Therle off hertford
my lorde admirall
The bisshop off duresme
The bisshop off winchester
Sir Jhon gage Comptrollour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsse
Sir Antony wingfelde vichawmberlain
Sir thomas wriothesley secretarie
Sir rauf Sadlair secretarye

The mayour and shirifes wyth diuerse off the aldermen
parsonally exhibited an inforamtion ageynst to the cownsell
ageynst on Jheronimo a strawnger dwelling wythin the hows off
the late gray friars / wherin they alleged ageynst the sayde
Jheronimo aswell sondry his lewde misbehauoures contrarye
to the common order off the realme / as his resistence ageynst
the kinges officers attempting apon probable suspitions the
serching off his hows / whereapon an order was taken
that the sayde Jheronimo sholde appere before the pryvye
cownsell the tuisday next after the date hereoff / Att
whiche tyme suche sholde likewise appere as cowlde iustifye
the forsayde accusation pretended as is abouesayde etc.
