An information wyth certeyne depositions war sent
from the mayour and iurates off Sandewyche towching
certeyne vnfitting wordes spoken by on Thomas Mylbanke
who thesame day was committed to the portoures warde there
to contynew vntyll he sholde trye owt the authour off the
sayde wordes / whiche he promised to do /
