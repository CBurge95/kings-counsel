The vth day of december being present as
the day before

Forasmoche as Frawnces Lilgraue widow had in open communicacion
sklawndarid the lady anne of cleues, and towchid allso therein the
sklawndar of the kinges parson she affirming to haue hard the same
off other and yett cowlde bring forth no authour off the same, she
was for her ponisshement committed to the towar

And the same day richard tavernour on of the clarckes of the signet
both for the concealing off the sayde sklawnder hard off hir and
other and reporting allso thesame himself was committed liekwise
to the towar


The vjth day off december att westminster
being than present att christeschirch place
being than present

A letter was sent to Master solliciter at horssham the duches off Norffolk
hows to putt allthing there in a safe custodye and all to to
putt all the keis off euery sevarall warde in som one coder and to bring
the key thereoff wyth him Leaving charge in his absence to Master carrell
dwelling therebye, and willing him now and than to repayre thither
to se nothing other en remouid or embesieled, till the kinges plesir
sholde be farther knowen on this behalff
