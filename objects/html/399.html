Master dacres

Whereas an information was exhibited to the cownsell and
depositions of thesame ageynst one Antonye hutchinson
souldiour off guisnes for the speaking off certeyne lewde
and vnsemely worddes Letters Forasmoche as itt appered
that he was dronke whom he speake thesame / Letters wer
written to Sir Jhon Walloppe / after a good Leasson
and exhortation to dismisse him / and to sett him
ageyne at libertie
