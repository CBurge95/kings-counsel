The vijth viijth ixth and xth
daye the king removed to london
with his pryvey chamber only
leving behynd hym here at
hampton courte the Quenes
grace with the hole household
& retorned agayn the xth daye


At Hampton courte the xjth of
february beyng present the Counsail
sat did not sitte /


At hamptoncourte the xijth of
february / beyng present
The gret chamberlayn of England
The Comptroller of household
The Master of the horses
The vicechamberlayn
Sir Thomas Wryothesly Secretary

They pervsed aswel certayn letteres sent aswel from thirle of
hertford & syr Edward Kerne knight tooching the
matters in veryance for codde cowbridge and the couswade
as also se other letteres sent from the sayd Erle and the
lorde depute & others of the kinges Counsail there
at Calais concernyng certain treasons committed
by one syr Robery Harvie prise Comissery of the
sayd towne whichwer reveled by one Broket
and sir Thomas Tey priste curat of sainte
Maries churche in the sayd towne /
