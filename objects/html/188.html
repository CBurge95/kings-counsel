At Grenewhich the thirde day of
Maye / being present /
The gret ChamberLain of Englande
The bisshop of Duresme
The Treasorer of household
The Comptroler of household
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretary

Gregory a Smyth who was beingwas sent for by priuey Seale appered
before the said Counsaill / and was examyned of this same> matier
wherof that Richard Germyn was sent for examined the daye before did agree in every thing
with that the said Germyn had confessed before the daye before /
And forasmuchas the thinges layed to the sayd Germyns
charge semed to haue proceded rather of lightnes negligence then of any evill
entent or purpose / wer bothe dimissed having a good lesson
gyven vnto them they wer both discharged /

Robert Sandewhich priste who was bounde by a recognisaunce as appereth
the xiijth day of Aprill last past / was discharged of
the said recognisaunce /

It was agreed that syr Robert Sandwich prist shuld
be discharged of his recognisaunce wherin he was bounde
as appereth the xiijth of Aprill last past / in the
xxxijth yere of the kinges highnes Reign /
