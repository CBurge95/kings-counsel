A Letter was written to Master wylliam pagett the kinges
highnes embassadour in fraunce / in the behalff of wylliam
wylford and other marchawntes / requiring so to sett forward
theyre matter / as brief expedition off iustice might ensue accordingly

A minute off thind a payre off indentures betwixt the kinges and
the staplers was redde / conteynyng aswell the kinges highnes
license for the shipping off theyre wooddes for oon yere rekning from
the first day of marche tyll the last day off februarye next
then ensuing / as the convenantes off the sayde staplers for the
paying the kinges returne att callais the sixt day off Aprill
and the sixt day off october forasmoche as they sholde shippe / And
thesame was determined to putt in parchement /

Att westminster the third day off Marche
being than present
The duke off Norffolk
The Duke of Suffolk etc of as the day before

Whereas oon sympson the parson off farly had exhibited to
the cownsell a certeyne byll conteynyng heynows and traytorows wordes
spoken as it was pretend surmised / by oon Jhon ownstende off oxstede
reporting the sayde wordes as spokenby a brother off his called
Jhon ownstende off sawnderstede / this day aswell the sayde Jhon ownstede
off oxstede appereid before the cownsell as his brother Jhon ownstede
off sawnderstede / And both off them denyyng that euer they spake
any suche wordees / yett forasmoche as the sayde Jhon ownstede off
oxstede was convinced by iiij wytnesses / that is to say symson
parson off farly Jhon ested and his wiffe J & Jhon bassett
and his wife / he was committed to the towar and his brother
bownde in recognisance as after ensueth

The condition off this recognisance is this that if thabouebownden
Jhon ownsted / do appere att all tymes vpon a reasonable warning
before the priuye cownsell / and there make answar to all suche
thinges as shall be layed vnto his charge than this recognisaunce
to stande voyde or ells etc. /

The condiditon of this recognisance is suche as if that whereas
Antonio gaidotti florentyne stondeth now indetted to the kinges highnes
in certayne summes off money / as appertith by sondrye speciales
remayning in the handes off Sir brian tuke knight thresorour
of the kinges chawmber / In cace the sayde Antonio pay or cawse
to be payde all the sayde dett wythin one yere after the date hereoff
or ells wythin the sayde terme bring in for the same syche suretyes
as it shall please the kinges highnes to accept / or ells before the
last day off the sayde on yere rendre his bodye ageyne to
prison / than this recognisance to stande voyde / or ells & c.
