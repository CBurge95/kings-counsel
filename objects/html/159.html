A lettere was writtento the Baillifes of St Albons to kepe
in sure custodye a certain woman which had confessed before
syr Fraunces Bryan knight to haue which that she had spoken certain vnsuting
woordes touching the kinges Maieste and his highnes Counsail
and to knowe of examyne her where and of whom she had herd the said woordes
further and further therupon to aduertise the Counsall of their
procedinges in the same /
