At westminster the xxiiijth off nouember being
than present
Tharchebisshop of Cantorbury
The lord chawncellour
The duke off Norffolk
The lorde priuye seale
The lord great Chawmberlayne
Therle of Hertford
The high admirall of inglond
The bisshop of winchester
Sir Jhon gage Comptroller
Sir Antony browne Master of the hors
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretarye
Sir rafe Sadlair secretary

Whereas Jhon grevill esquier vpon an heynowse offense pretendid aginsst
him was sent for from hamtoncourt by a sergeant at armys to appere befor
the cownsell, this day he made his apperance, and r bynding hymselff in
a recognisance whiche herafter ensueth to appere from day to day was
dismissed from the custody of the sayde sergeant

The condition of this recognisance is such that if thabouebownden Jhon
grevell do dewlye from day to day gyve his attendaunce vpon the cownsell
attending on the kinges parson, vntill such tyme as he shall by them
dismissed than the sayde recognisance to stonde voyde or ells to stonde
in full strength and vigour

A warrant was stampid of the date off the date of the xvth of Nouember
for the deliuerye of Ljli vs viiijd to richard Musgraue esquier capitayne
of the northen sowdiurs at guisnes for the payment off his owne wages
and his soldiurs for certayne days etc.

A warrant was stampid to Tristran Tesshe for the deliuerie to
dame Elsabeth Nevell, of all g suche gooddes and cattalles as wer
apperteyning to sir Jhon Nevell knight at the tyme of his attachement
Siche sommis only deductid owt of thesame as had bin spent
in the convayance of the sayde sir Jhon hither and thither ageyne

Letters wer signed to callais, and to Irland


Att westminster the xxvth day off Nouembir
