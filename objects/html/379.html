Whereas one Oliuer Russell saltpetermaker / for misdeaw
ning himselff wyth lewde wordes toward the deane off Canterbury
and sondrye other there was at westminster by order off the
cownsell committed to the marshallsee This day being sent
for from thens bownde in recognisance wyth two sureties for
his good abearing to the sayde parties after a good leasson
was sett ageyne att libertie

The condition off this recognisance is thus / that if thaboue
bownden Oliuer do appere before the cownsell ocotauis
hillarij / And in the meane season kepe the peace to the deane
off canterbury and his seruantes / Or ells do repayre thither
and aske thesame forgyunesse than etc. or ells etc.

A placart was signed by the cownsell for forster
to take vppe horses by post to barwycke /
