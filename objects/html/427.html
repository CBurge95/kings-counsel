Att hamptoncourt the xviijth day off
Januarye being than present
The lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hertfford lord priuye seale
The lorde saint John
The bisshop off westminster
Sir thomas cheyney treasorour
Sir Antony browne Master off the horsses
Sir Antony wingfelde vicehamberlain
Sir thomas wriothesley secretary
Sir richard riche chawncellour off the augmentations

Whereas monsir de bies beyng refused att the handes off
the lorde deputye off calais to convay certeyne wyne for the vittailing
off Arde by the kinges new riuer att guisnes, menassid to stay
therefore the comming off vittailles in to callais / Letteres wer written
to the saide lorde deputye to declare vnto the saide monsie de bies
that the k his maieste had made this riuer for a fortresse, and
that as itt was no reason for fortresses to be commen passages
so wolde he nott permitt the saide riuer to be vsed in any other
sorte /
