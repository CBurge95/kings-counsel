kinges Nauye vpon the narrow sees / that where as he hadde
stayed certeyne shippes off portugall whoom wer suspected
to be summe frenchemennes gooddes / he sholde vse all th good
wayes and meanes for the triall off thesame For the whyche
purpose was sent vnto him one peter Neming mariner
who cowlde somwhat declare therein

William Tussard hauing ben committed vnto the fleete
for sondrye disseytes obiected vnto him to be committed in his
thexcercise off his office off keping off the seale off the kinges
benche was this day deliuered and bounde by recognisaunce
toyth two suerties after the sorte as ensueth

The condition off this recognisance is suche that whereas
thabouebounden william is accused to haue deceyued the kinges highnes
sondrye sommes off money / in thexcerciseng off his office
in the kinges benche / if the sayde william be att all tymes
forthcomming to pay all suche fynes as he shall be condemmed
therefore to pay / by the kinges most honorable cownsell
tha netc. or elles etc.

The onciftion of this recognisance is suche as if thabouebounden
Leonard and Roger do att all tymes wythin six monthes
next ensuing vpon all any convenient warning present
the bodye off wylliam Tussard before the kinges most
honorable counsell than etc. or ells etc.

prouision off billett to be sent to be sent to barwyke
wyth aduertisement that brewars bakers and coopers
so many as sholde be nedefull for the baking and brewing
off biskett and bere sholde be sent from hens thither /


pagebreak


The compaye companye off brewars wer before
the cownsell / and wer commawnded to appoynt vj habel
men and expert in brewing to be sent to barwyke
thesame to be presented on monday next before the
cownsell / in suche ordre as if nede sholde be they
might departe on tewesday /

The company off Coopers wer commawnded to bring in
writing vpon monday next what nombre off barrelles
and constrelles they wer hable to furnisshe wythin
vj wekes / and to appoynt allso vj hable men off
theyre occupation to be sent to barwyke /

The companye off bakers wer like wyse commaunded
to appoynt vj hable and expert men off theyre
occupation to be presented before the cownsell on
monday next / thesame to be sent to barwyke
