A lettere was sent to the lord chawncellour to direct commissions
to all sherefes and iustices off peace thorowghowt the realme
to make musters incontinentlye / and certifye the nomber

A lettere was sent to sir Antony kingston to desist and
ceasse from ministering and taking any man wythin the
forrest off deane

The same day a lettere was directed to gennyns viceadmirall
of the shipps that now be on the see declaring vnto him
the kinges highnes pleser for the ordering of suche men of
war as he sholde probably perceyue to be robbers on the see
or to haue ben busye wythin the kinges stremes or otherwyse
so to houure and lye before the costes hannes or crakes off
off englonde as the trade off marchandise myght and entre in
to the sayde haures might therbye be empeched ot interrupted /
