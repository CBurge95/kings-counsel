Att hamptoncourt the xiiijth day off
May being than present as the day
before /

A lettere was written to the president off the marches off
wales to se master Mylton being appoynted to be on off the kinges
highnes commissioners to haue suche entreteynement as in suche
cases haue ben accustomed

The kinges highnes plesir was declared towching rewardes
to be geven to Jacke a musgraue / Thomas dacres / Eglanbye
Blanechasett / and the greynes /

A lettere was written to the president off th the cownsell
in yorcke for thexpedition off hungates parces according vnto
iustice

A lettere was written to the duke off Suffolcke to se the fees
dew for thexcercising of thoffices off the surveyourship off barwyke
the receyuourshippe off Shereshutton Myddleham and barnard
castell to thomas Gower appoynted by the kinges highnes
to the same / And to assigne allso vnto him convenient allowance
for the portage off the kinges treasour thither / And farther to
th take order that he may be payde for thoffice he excercised
in sir george lawsons dayes / vntyll Ester last past /

A lettere was written to the Duke off Suffolcke that whereas
a certeyne shippe off Norway had ben lately stayed att barwyke / he
sholde with all good dexterite searche and trye what skottisshe mennes
gooddes myght be fownde wythin thesame / whiche hi the kinges
plesir was sholde be taken owt off the shippe and sequestred to his vse
the rest with the shippe to be redliuerd to the captayne wyth gentill
wordes accordinglie /

Letteres wer written to Sir Edward wotton treasourour off Callais
to receyue of Master geoffrey One off the clarckes off the pryuye seale
the summe of Six thowsand powndes to be employed abowt the payment
of the ordinary garrisons and the crew vndre his charge etc.

A lettere was written to Thomas palmer treasorour off the crew
and fortifications at guisnes to the receyue off the sayde master geoffray
six thowsande pownde to be employed abowght the fortifications and
the crew there /

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebonden
Mathew and James do appere before the cownsell on friday
fortnight after the date hereoff than etc. or ells etc.

Vpon an information exhibited ageynst Christofor Assheton
of the countie off barkshere gentleman / by certeyne off the kinges highnes
subgectes towching his misdemeanour among them namely for exacting
vnlawfully in the musters certeyne contributions among them for the
grounding off horsses / and the taking off money off summe off them
for theyre tarriing att home / A commission was sent vnto Sir
wylliam Estsex knight and Alexandre Vmpton Esquier to examyne
diligenteley the hole trewth off the matter and to aduertise thesame wyth
spede /

A lettere was written to Master Stanhop to pike owt iiij or v
off the most substantiall off all the frenchemen remayning prisoners
att hull and to dismisshe dismisse the rest vpon theyre rawnsoms
to be payed in caec we sholde grow to warre /

A lettere was written to the duke off suffolk in the fovem
off James wierstrop repayring in to skotlande by ordre
of certeyne marchauntes off the stylliard for the recouuray off
a shippe taken by the frenchemen and convayed in to skotland

Two commissions wer signed wyth the stamp for Antony
Auchar appoynted to se a proportion of wood convayed with
spede to callais / to take vppe byllett and logges in all
places night vnto the water syde in kent and Estsex

A lettere was sent to tharchebishop off yorcke to stay the disposi
tion off the Stewardship off hexham being off his gyft
and to graunt thesame to thassignement off the kinges highnes

Letteres wer sent to Antony Auchar Auchar towching the
prouision off the sayd wood and money to be laid layed
owt by him for the frayght thereoff / and the owners off the
woodde to be remitted hither for theyre money

A lettere was sent to the mayour off Cambridge to repayre
hither on friday fortnight after the date hereoff /

A lettere was sent allso to my lorde deputye off callais
and to the rest of the kinges cownsell there for the restitution
of Sir Thomas Palmers gooddes remayning in the handes
off Sir Antony Knevett and sondry other persons sithen the
tyme off his apprehension /

Letteres wer deuised and sent to master wallop to know off
him in cace the kinges highnes sholde sende ovar vjc horssemen
more what forrage and horssemeete he sholde be hable to furnisshe
there /

A lettere was deuised and sent to John Fletcher off Rye
that whereas he hadd stayed iij flemmyshe shipps laden wyth
wynes comming owt off fraunce he sholde incontinently dismisse
them / constreyning them to break no bulcke nether to sell
any parte thereoff vnlesse they sholde wyllingly condestende
therevnto and hereoff hereafter in no cace to stay any
suche from being off themperowres subgectes from whens
so ever they sholde coom or wherewyth so ever s they shodle
be laden /

Letteres wer sent to Master Sadlair towching therle
off Casseilles and the sheref of Ayre to be agreed by his
good help and meane /

A lettere was written to the president off the marches off wales
etc towching a certeyne contention betwene the Erle off worcester
and the lorde ferrers abowght the mustering off meane to be
ordred by his discretion /

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thabouebonden
Arnolde do restore and redeliuer to the kinges vse all suche
gowlde or other treasour as hauing ben taken owt off the
frencheprise in myldeforde hauen is coom in to his handes / or
shall hereafter by any meane coom in to his possession / and do
aduertise from tyme to tyme off all suche parsons as he shall
hire of know to haue in theyre possessions any of the saide gowlde
than etc. orells etc. /
