Letteres were sent to my lord Chauncelour aduertising
sending vnto hym of the two de letteres patentes of denyzens
which wer sollen owte and also of the charge that Arnold
Albert and his felowe had to appere before hym to purge them
selfes in that mater /

Letteres were sent to Thomas Fouler of Calais for the
payment vnto Guysnes of his wages vntil the kinges pleasir
to the contrary wer knowen

Letteres sent to the president & counsail in the marches of wales
declaring the kinges pleasir tooching the admission of John
ap rice to be secretary there and Edmund Fox & Charles
Fox to be Clerkes of the Signet furthwith and clerkes
of the counsail when the same shuld fall voyde /
