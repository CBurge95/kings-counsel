Whereas a contention had long depended betwene
the baylifes off drotwhich in the countye off worcestour as Sir
richard Cornewell preist abowght certeyne proffites incident
to the seruice off saint richard in the towne abouesayde
forasmoche as itt appered be depositions that the sayde sir
richard was by then herein wronged / an order was taken
that the sayde baylifes sholde permitt the sayde sir richard
peasablye to enioy the saide pro sirvice with all proffittes
and comodites therevnto belonging / And for the perfor
maunce hereof the knowleged to one the king eche off them on
hundreth pownde and every then the hole in manner and
sorte as hereafter followeth

The condition off this recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bounden Thomas, george, Jhon and Jhon and eche of them do
permitt and suffer (and doo that shall lye in them that other
pretending interest shall do thesame) Sir Richard Cornewell
preist to enioy the seruice off of saint richard in the towne
off droitwyche in the cowntye off worcetour according to the kinges
maiestes gift with all proffittes commodites and aduantages suche
as other enioying thesame siruice haues had in possession / in
whyche proffites are accounted fowre bullaries off salt, whereoff
whereoff the pristes sirving in the sayde sirvice haue ben in
possession as by certeyne depositions off witnes doth appere

pagebreak


from the first off christemas next comming vnto suche
tyme as the burgesses off the sayde towne haue by sute
off petition to the kinges maieste obteyned declaration off
the pretended title to the gift off the sayde sirvice And farther
that for on yere to ende at Christemas next comming
the sayde parties pay or cawse to be payed to the sayde
Sir richard Cornewell before the feast off Saint Andrew
next comming the summe off vjli xiijs iiijd for and in the
name off the sayde fowre bullaries accustomed to be payed
to the preist occupiing that seruice Thirse two articles
fully obserued and kept the recognisance to to be voyde
and any oon off them nott fulfilled thesame to stonde
in full strength and effect

Whereas vpon a contention betwene one william bulmer esquier
and Jone his wife / the sayde william absenting himselff from
her wythowt any reasonable occasion and allowing her
nothing wherewyth to entreteyne herselff notwythstonding
he might be her dispende one hundreth marckes by yere /
An order was betwene them taken by the cownsell as appereth here before at
westminster the Forasmoche as the sayde william
most contemptuowslye disobeyed the sayde order / Letters
wer written to the president off the cownsell in the north to
sequester his landes / and to cawse the receyuours thereoff
from tyme to tyme to sende the same to master mason Clarcke
of the cownsell to thentent thesame might be betwene
then apportioned as to reson and equite sholde
apperteyne

Vpon an information sent by sir Jhon walloppe towching
thonlawfull exaction off heddemoney by the baylif of guisnes for all beastes taken wythin
by bowtyes and seolde wythin thenglisshe ple pale letteres
wer written to my lorde deputye and Antony rows

have vsed to do the like / in whiche cace reason wolde that
he sholde do thesame Otherwise they sholde take order aswell
that he sholde hereafter surceasse to make any suche
vnlawfull clayme / as to make immediate restitution
of that he hath receyued receyued allredy vnder this
pretence /

Letteres wer sent to sir Jhon walloppe declaring the
kinges good acceptation of his procedinges / and sygnifying to
him thatt his maiesties pleser was that all suche prisoners as sholde
be taken wythin the pale sholde be restored frankelye
and frelye wythowt rawnsom to be payed to the taker

Whereas the rome of the gouuernour off the felowship
off marchauntes aduenturors being voyde / And one or
two successyvelye chosen and named therevnto / refused
neverthelesse thesame / The companye off the sayde fellowship
att Antwerp wrote theyre letteres to the others here desiring
them being wyse and graue men and off great
experience , to name summe one whome they sholde and
wolde elect to the rome abouesayde Who accordinglye
calling here theyre court two seuerall tymes condescendid
at the length vpon named Casteline / whome
being in theyre opinions a rigght mete man for the
saide purpose / the required the sayde companye at Antwarp
to ratifie and elect for gouuernour according to theyre
promes In whose commendation allso at that tyme
the kinges highnes priuye cownsell wrote theyre
favorable letteres Forasmoche as nether regarding
the keping off theyre promes, nether yett hauing
suche respect as they owght to haue hadde to the marchauntes
here being theyre heddes and masters they chase and
elected this notwithstonding one knotting a man

pagebreak


of suche qualites / as no man was to be thowght
more vnmete therefore. Thys day in the name
off all the rest entrede theyr complaynt hereoff Sir
Richard gressham / powle withepowle perpoynte
and gressham And declaring vnto the
cownsell aswell the worcke receyued by them att
the handes off the sayde yongmen resident in
Antwerppe / as the contempt allso evidentyle shewed
off the cownseilles letteres they desired humblye summe
ramedie to be deuised on this behalff whereapon
letteres wer deuised and sent to thesayde fellowshipes
at antwerppe requiring aswell in respect of
the sayde considerations as in consideration allso
that the sayde knotting was noted to be
an h inhabitant off the sayde towne off Antwerp
as and as itt was thowght / a freman off thesame /
to retorne to a new election / and in thesame to
elect and name the sayde Castelye according
to theyre former promes / or ells the sayde
knotting and the clarcke off the fellowshippe to
repayre immediatelye to the courte / and to present
themselffes with all convenient diligence before the
priuye cownsell attending vpon the kinges highnes
most royall parson wyth all convenient diligence
