The condition of this recognisance is suche that if thaboue bownden
rauf do appere at any tyme in mense pasthe next ensuing
before the priuye cownsell / and from the day off his apperance
do gyve his contynuall attendance vntyll suche tyme as he
shall be by them dismissed than etc. or ells etc.

A Lettre was deuised to Jhon daniel esquier for to del sende
or bring to the cownsell as all suche writinges as remayned
in his custodye towching his late wyfes landes now apperteyning
to Jhone bulmer /

Certayne men off the towne off yorke appering before the cownsell
sayd vnto thesame for the repealing off the proclamation made
in hull by the kinges commawndement towching forrayn bowght and
forrayne solde And after sondry allegations and reasons declared
by them how ioyfull the sayde proclamation was to the hole contrey
the cownsell resolued to declare thesame to the kinges highnes / and therevpon
to make thereoff att a convenient leasure a resolute answer /
