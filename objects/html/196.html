The xxiiijth day of May the
Counsail went from Grenewhich
to Westminster and there sat it
in Counsail in the Starre Chamber

Thomas Twhaytes who had bene prisoner in the Tower for
spekeng of certain lewde woordes nerer touching the kinges Maieste
was brought before the Counsail who and having a good lesson
gyven hym to vse his tong with more discecion herafter was dimissed
and set at liberty /
