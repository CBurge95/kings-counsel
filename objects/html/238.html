At Scrowby the xvijth
letteres day of August being present /
the same that wer the day
before

Two severall letteres wer written to Master Chauncelour
of the Tenthes and Master Moyle Signifying vnto
them that the kinges Maieste had appoynted assigned them
to be commissioners at Calais for the Sirvey &
ordering of certain thinges there Which his Maieste would have
done there and also therfore requyring them to put themselfes
in such arredynes as they might vpon aduertisement of the kinges
Maiestes further pleasyr to be gyven vnto then for that
purpose repayre thither accordingly /


At Hatfeld the xviijth day of August
the Counsail did not sitt /


At Hatfeld the xixth day of August
being present
The Duke of Norffolk
The Duke of Suffolk
The gret Chambrelain of Englande
The gret Admiral of Englande
The Treasorer of household
The Comptroller of household
