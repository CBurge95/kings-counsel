There was also a Lettere to William lucye of chorlerote in the
Countye of Warlock gentleman that forasmocheas he went about by
sinistre meane to expelle the sayd Thomas Oldney out of the
sayd ferme without iust cause or consideracion having before tyme
vnto the sayd Oldney as he affirmeth that he is able
to prove by witnesse he the sayd lucye shuld therfor
eyther permit the sayd Olbay to enioye the sayd ferme
and to make vnto hym a sufficient assurance of the same
or eles appere personally b furthwith before the said counsail
to make further answer in this behaulf as to Justice shuld
apperteyne /

Master Chauncelour of thaugmentacions & master pollard were
sent for to repayre to the courte at pipewell or coliweston
at the furthest and so to contynew theyr attendaunce
vppon the kinges Maieste vntil his maiestes Retorne all this
progresse /
