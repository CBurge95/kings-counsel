Whereas vpon sondry accusations exhibited and declared Ageynst the
ladye brygewater, she was therevpon committed to ward, so to contynew tyll
farther processe shold be made in that behalff according to equite and iustice
she hauing on dawghter and twoo sonnes whome in the meane season the kinges
highnes wold to be honestly bestowed, iij sondrye letters wer deuised off declaring
his graces pleser herin on to the lady off oxforth for the receyuing off the
dowghter, the other ij, to tharchebisshop off canterbury and the bisshop of duresme /
for the ij sommes requiring them and every off them to receyue these thre
children as they wer appoyntid, and to vse them towching theyre fynding
and entreteynement, as to theyre wisomes and discretions sholde be
thowght convenient

Letters wer sent to Master pagett the kinges ambassadour in the court off
fraunce declaring to him the namys off certayne personages committed to warde
vpon, the quenes lamentable cace, and therewythall the qualices off theyre defawghtes

A warrant vnder stampe was directid to sir brian tuke for the payment off xxli
vjs viiijd fro to william homming for somoch money defrayed for the kinges hows
to the sayde william etc.
