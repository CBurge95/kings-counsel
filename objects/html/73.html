At Wyndesour the xviijth of
Novembre being present
The Duke of Suffolk
The Bishop of Duresme
The Lord Chamberlain to the king
The Comptroller of household
The vicechambrelain
Sir Thomas Wriothesley knight Secretary

Twoo letteres of apperance vnder the stampe wer sent to sir John
Seintclo knight and william Seintclo his sonne and heyre to appere
before the king and his Maiestes priuey Counsail to make aunswer
vnto such thinges as at their cummyng shuld be obiected vnto
them by Thomas Kemys and to kepe the peax themselfes & at
theirs against the said Kemys /


At wyndesour the xixth of Nouembre
being present /
The Duke of Suffolk
The gret Admiral of England
The Bishopp of Duresme
The Lord Chambrelain of Eng to the king
The Comptroler of household
The vicechambrelain
Sir Thomas Wriothesley knight Secretary

A lettere vndir the Stampe was written to the President of the Counsaill in the marches
of wales and sir Nicolas Hare knight in such forme as was one
directed vnto them the xvjth of Octobre /
