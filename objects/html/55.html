At wyndesour the xxjth of October
beyng present
the lord privey Seale
The Comptroller of household
The Master of the horses
The vicechamberlayn
Sir Rafe Sadler Secretary

William Harrys of Essex wrote sirvant browght a lettere from
his sayd Master vnto the Counsail aduertising the same that
wheras the had receyved a lettere from them to put in good
keping a certayn hoye laden with buttre & chese & stayd as
forfait in the water of burnary he had before the recepte
of their sayd lettere del suffre the owners therof to passe
a way with the same by the commaundement of the Duke of
Norfolk his letteres directed vnto hym by his letteres for that
behaulf /

William Gaywood & Thomas Gaywood vndersircheours
in Essex which befo wer sent for to appere before the lordes
& others of the privey Counsail appered & wer commaunded
to attend vntil they will here at the court vpon the sayd Counsail from tyme to tyme vntil they wer
called for /

Letteres wer browght from the Counsail in Irland to the
kinges highnes aduertising the same his Maieste of only of
such theyr proceding as his highnes was aduertised of
before by letteres sent from the depute the xijth of September
and at this tyme also was browght a certayn actes
in parchement ap vndre the gret seale of Irland which they
defend l myght be retorned vnto them agayn vndre the
grete seale of England /

Letteres wer browght to the Counsail from Master pate the
abstract wherof doth ensue /

Wayvodes brother the monk was arumed in buda to with
stand king ferdinand /

Monsir de pelon shuld go from themperour to the french king
aswel to appease the disp excuse the fawte which was layd to
his his capitaynes in prement for brekyng the places & as also
to excuse the enhauncement of the price of salt in
burgendy wherwith the frenche king was displeased & to
the sayd french king ad colloqum for the sayd purposes /


pagebreak


Duke philip Frederick had promised to themperour
to cause the Duke of Cleves to cum eftesones to hym &
for that purpose was noew repayred to the sayd Duke

Yong garret of Irland & ij pedagoges with hym
wer in a Cloyster besides liege & there reaported hym
self to be the kinges next heyre /

Brawnsetre was gone to rome one beyng his guyde
that had bene doctor augustyns sirvant

Shipperns that was wont to be sent so moche to the
turke hawnted often Monsir de Velyes howse the frenche
ambassadour there /


At wyndesour the xxijth of October
beyng present
The Lord pryvey Seale
The Comptroller of household
The Master of the horses
The vicechambrelain
Sir Rauff Sadler knight Secretarye /

The Lord gret master was sent for by a lettere vndir the stampe to be
before the feast of Allhalontyde at the courte

William Harrys of Essex had gyven vnto him by a lettere bothe
thankes for his siruice doon vnto the king in those parties / and alsoo
a commaundement / to keep safe to be furth cummyng certain butter & chese / which
his siruant enformed the Counsaill that he had yet remainyng
in his Custodye /
