Duke philip Frederick had promised to themperour
to cause the Duke of Cleves to cum eftesones to hym &
for that purpose was noew repayred to the sayd Duke

Yong garret of Irland & ij pedagoges with hym
wer in a Cloyster besides liege & there reaported hym
self to be the kinges next heyre /

Brawnsetre was gone to rome one beyng his guyde
that had bene doctor augustyns sirvant

Shipperns that was wont to be sent so moche to the
turke hawnted often Monsir de Velyes howse the frenche
ambassadour there /
