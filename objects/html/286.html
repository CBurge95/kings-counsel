Att Christchirche place the viijth ixth of december
present as before

Sir Jhon gostwicke knight Jhon skynner war appoynted
to go to reigate to in lord lord williams hows there to take a trew and iust
inventorye off all siche money, iewelles, gooddes, and cattalles as
they sholde there fynde and to bryng the sayde inventory to the cownsell

Master Secretarie wriothesley, master pollard and Master attourney
wer appoyntyd, to go to lambhith to the duches off Norffolk hows and
my lorde williams hows for like puirpos

Sir richard long and Sir thomas pope wer appoynted to go
to my lady brigewaters hows in kent and sowthwarcke for like
pourpos

This day the lorde william after a generall examination was
committed to the tower

Itt was determined that the next day following culpeper and
derham sholde suffer onles the king sholde sende contrarie word
and thereoff wer letters dispatchid to his highnes
