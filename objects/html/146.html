A lettere vndir the Stampe was written to the Custumers
Comptrollers Surveyours and syrchours of the porte of Southampton
to permitt and suffre George foonder syruant to the lorde
Matravers Deputy of the towne & Marches of Calais
to Lade and cary away from the said porte to the towne
of Calais two hundred quarters of wheate for the only store of
the sayd deputyes household byndyng takyng of the sayd pouder
in a sufficient bonde to bring or sende by a convenyent day a certificat vnder the Seales
of the sayd of the Deputy and one other of the counsail there
that he hath delyvred the sayd CC quarters of wheate
at the sayde towne accordyngly
