Att westminster the xixth day off Marche
being than present
Tharchebisshop off canterbury
The duke off Norffolk
Therle lorde russell lord priuye seale
Therle off hartford
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
The lorde saint John
Sir Jhon gage
Sir Antony browne
Sir Antony wingfelde
Sir thomas wriothesly secretary

Letteres wer written and sent to Sir wylliam
west knyght to repayre from plymmowth to the downes
and there to remayne vntyll he sholde farther know the
kinges plesur

A lettere was allso written to the mayour and thinhabitauntes
off hull to conteyne from the chowsing off Sir wylliam knowles
to the rome off the mayraltie / in respect off the charge
off the Custummershippe there / to thexcercise whereoff he
was assigned /

Morbacke inhabitant off the towne off windesour
For certeyne seditious opinions and other his misbehauours in
defense of the and mayntenaunce off thesame was committed
to the Marsshallsee /

A lettere was sent signed by the stampe to the deane bisshop and
chaptre off exetre to certifie what they knew towching the evyll
opinions off doctour haynes /

An ordre was taken that off the xxiijli receyued off bulmers
landes from the bisshop off landaff by the handes off the clarcke off
the cownsell / xxli sholde be deliuered to Johans bulmer wife
to wylliam bulmer esquier by the sayde clarcke and the rest
to be sent to wylliam bulmer remayning in the flete
