Att westminster the xviijth day off
octobre being than present
Tharchebisshop off Canterbury
The lrode chawncellour
Therle off hertford
The bisshop off winchester
The bisshop off westminster
Sir thomas wriothseley secretary
Sir rafe Sadlair secretary
Sir richard riche
Sir Jhon baker

Vpon an information exhibited by the mayour and
aldermen of Callais ageynst on robart pole mayour
baylif off Cobham pretending the keping off a court
late to be incident to his office / whiche had all way been
vsed to be kept by the sayde mayour and aldremen
forasmoche as they sent the the charter off king Edward
the third for theyre title on this behalff appered
to be iust / Letters war written to the lorde deputye
to se the mayour and aldermen abouesayde to be herein
no more molested butt that they might peasable
enioy the keping off the sayfde cowrte vntill the sayde
pole sholde here appere and declare wherefore they
owght nott so to do /
