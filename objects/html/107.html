Letteres wer written to the Mayer Aldermen Baillifes and
commons of the Towne of Oxford to send vp at the
next terme a personage s authorised by writting vnder
their towne seael to here what ordir shalbe taken by the
Lord Chauncelour Lord Priuey Seale and other of the
kinges counsaill for the matyers of controuersye betwene
them and thuniversyty
