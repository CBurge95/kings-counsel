Letters wer sent to the president off the cownsell at
yorcke to receyue the lorde kilmars, the lorde maxwell
wyth other skottisshe prisoners at the handes of the lorde
scrope / and thesame so receyued to committ to Sir henrye
Savell and to Sir thomas wentworth to be conveyed
by them safelye to the kinges highnes

Letters wer allso sent to the duke of norffolk declaring
vnto him the order taken with master stanhop for the prouision
off certeyne grayne aswell presentlye as ageynst marche
next ensuing /
