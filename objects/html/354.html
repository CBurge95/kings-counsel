The condition off this recognisance is suche as if
thabouebownden Edmunde and Charles do gyve theyre
attendaunce vpon the cownsell attending vpon the kinges
most royall parson / every sonday vntill they shall

Att hamptoncourt the seconde off
Julye being present vt supra

The condition of this recognisance is suche as if thabouebownden
Jhon gregorie and thomas Crowe, theyre heyres and ex
excutoures pay or cawse to be payed vnto the thresorour off the
kinges chawmber for the tyme the summe off xl li in suche
forme as herafter ensueth that is to say att midsomer
next after the date hereof x li and so yerely at the sayde
feast in like manner vntyll the hole summe off the sayde
xl li shall be fully payed than etc or ells etc.

Att hamptoncourt the viijth off
Julye present as before

Sir richard whitford Aphowell committed to the fleete
xvij Junij by the lorde chawncellour was this day dismissed
bownde first in recognisance as followeth

The condition of this recognisance is suche as if thaboue
bownden Richard aphowell do vpon a lawfull warning att
any tyme wythin on yere after the date hereoff appere
immediately before the priuye cownsell and there gyve his
attendaunce till he be by then dismissed then etc or
ells etc

The condition of this recognisance is suche as if the sayde
maurice dennys appering before the chawncellour of thaug
mentations do follow and abyde suche order as the sayde
chawncellour shall determin bet for the ende off a contention
betwene the sayde maurice and Master hainege for a certeyne
marche marsshe belonging to the late hospitall of saint Jhons
than etc. or ells etc. /
