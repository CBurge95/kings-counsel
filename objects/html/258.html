Whereas diuerse men off Skarburgh had suid to the
king for the gyft of certayne woodde, for the building and
repayring off the haven and the pirrys there . they had
for answer that on showlde be sent thither to vew
both the haven and wooddes and thereapon according to the
necessite of the havin, and as it might be thought the woodde
myght be sparid they sholde have to thyre petition a
resolute answer

This day was the priuye seale sent owt for Nicholas
poyntz knight
