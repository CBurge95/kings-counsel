A lettere was sent to wylliam gonston , to cawse a incontinently
a crayer to be laden wyth vittayle to be sent forthwyth in
to the downes for the furniture off Andrew flammocje capitayne
of the navye there and others /

Stevens parson off bentworth was sent vppe
hither by Oliuer wallop and george paullett by order off
the cownsell geven to them therefore / And being this day
examined he was remitted to his lodging vnder the warde
off suche as war sent vppe wyth him /

Thomas Weldon on of the masters off the howsholde
sent for to appere before the cownsell / being fownde
culpable in the maynteyning off one sir thomas parson
parson clarcke who was knowen to be a man off evill
