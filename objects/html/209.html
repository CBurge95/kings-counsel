Doctour Wootton the Kinges Ambassadour with the Duke of Cleves
was commaunded by a lettere vndir the Stampe at such tyme
as the saide Duke shuld be cum home again out of Fraunce
with gentle woordes to take his elave and to retorne home to
the kinges Maieste /

Two several letteres of one effect / wer sent to therle of
Cumbrelande and the lord Dacres requyring the same
to sende vppo aduertisement whither the right of fishing
about Carlisle belong to the Deputy Wardeyn of those
partyes or to the Capitain of Carlisle /

Lyonel Gray Porter of Berwik who was sent
for to aunswer to certain matyers which wer Layed
to his charge was dimissed for this tyme bicause the
letteres conteyning theffect of his accusacion wer not here
but in the keping of my Duke of Norffolk who absent
the said Lyonell being neverthles commaunded to giue
his attendaunce at York / at the Kinges Maiestes cummyng
thither /
