pollard / for the deliuerye off certeyne summes off money specified
in a bill annexed to the sayde warrant / to fraunces bassett widow

A Letter was deuised to Jhon androwys / myll, Thomas whight
warham wylliam kingesmill gentilmen for to trye and serche
owt the manner off liuing off certayne suspectid parsons /

Att westministir the xxiiijth of Januarye
being than present as the day before

The kinges highnes resolution was declared to the
staplers / whiche was that for oon yere next ensuing
they sholde excercise theyr trafique according as they haue
accustomed / making pay
ment to the retynew off callais
the first october and the
first off april

A warrant by stampe was conceyued by stame to sir brian
tuke that forasmoche as the keping off the castell off notingham
was by the kinges letters patentes gevin and grawnted to therle
off rutlande / that the sayde sir brian sholde for the payment
off any souldioures there deliuer no money to any other butt
to the sayde erle or the bringer off a letter for him towching
thesame

A warrant was conceyued by stampe to sir marten bowers
and mathew rowett masters off the kinges mynt for te conver
ting off ijmli sterling in to harp grotes
