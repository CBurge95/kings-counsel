Att westminster the iiij vth day off June
being than present
Tharchebishop off Canterbury
The lorde chawncellour off Englonde
The erle off hertford
The bisshop off westminster
The lorde saintJohn
Sir John gage Comptrollour
Sir richard riche
Sir John baker

A passeporte was signed for certeyne skottisshemen dismissed
in to skotlande /

The irisshe matters wer vewed / and parker secretarye to my
lorde deputye was commoned wyth all towching the same /

Letteres wer receyed from the mayour off dertmowth / who sent
allso wyth thesame a certeyne brittayne vnto the cownsail / whiche
required to speake wyth the king or ells wyth the cownsell /
And being presented vnto the sayde cownsell was commawnded
first to putt his matters in writing whiche don he was committed
to the deane off westminster


Att westminster the vjth off June being
present as before /

Obrien wyth the rest off the irisshemen browght in
theyre petitions to the cownsell to be perused and wayed
of thesame /

A lettere off answer was written to master treasourour declaring
vnto him that in cace he shoulde sende over any nombre off
horssemen or fotemen over and be side the nombre appoynted vnto
him they sholde entre in to wages accordinglye /


Att westminster the vijth off June being
present as before

Letteres wer receyed from therle off Shrewesbury / and therewithall
was sent vppe all a certeyne skott taken off him for a spye
