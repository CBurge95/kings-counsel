Letteres wer sent to master wallop aduertising hym of
the hole discourse that the kinges highnes did make
with the frenche ambassadour

Certayn Tyndall men of those that had bene owtlawes
arryve whose names do ensue

cam vp hither in the place & name of all such as had bene
owtlawes to submitt them selfes in such sorte to the kinges
mercy & grace as yt myght apper to the his Maieste that they wer
most repentant for their offences and wer deferred from makyng
their submission vntil such tyme as the kinges highnes cam
to wyndesour /
