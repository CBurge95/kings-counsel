A warrant was passed by stampe to Thomas Thomas derbye to take vppe
laborares etc for the settyng forward off his intent towching
the sowing making off wode /

Item a license to the staplers / for the shipping off wolles etc. for on yere

The condition of this recognisance is suche that if thabouebounden
Jhon Brereton do from tyme to tyme and from day to day gyve
his attendaunce vpon the cownsell attending vpon his the kinges
highnes parson / and nott departe vntill suche tyme as he shall
be by then licensid and dimissed then this recognisance to
stonde in none effect or ells to stonde in full strength and
vigeur

The condition of this recognisance is suche that if thabouebounden
Jhon brereton rauf kineston doo from tyme to tyme gyve his
attendaunce vpon the cownsell attending vpon the kinges highnes
parson / and do nott depart vntill he be by them dismissed
and licensed to thesame / than this recognisance to stande

The condition of this recognisance is suche that if thaboue
bownden Edward do from day to day gyve his attendance
vpon the cownsell attending vpon the most kinges honorable parson
than this recognisance to stande voyde or elles etc. /

Vpon a booke exhibited to the cownsell by certayne englisshe
marchawntes hawnting the trade of Danske / ageynst the rewlars
and inhabitauntes of thesame / aswell conteyning sondrie wronges suffred
off the sayde englisshe marchauntes contrarie to the owlde treatis
and priuileges / as requiring the sayde cownselles ayde for the
redresse off thesame / the marchawntes off the stilliard wer
commawnded this day tappere before the cownsell / And after
diuerse allegations / first that thinformations wer vntrew
and after that none remayned now in theyre companye that
wer inhabitantes of the sayde towne / yett forasmoche as itt was
declared vnto them that they being off thesame bodie wer
bownde in caces off iustice to make answer for the same / they
resolued immediately to wright theyre effections Letters yn this
behalff / and by the feast off whitsontide whiche now next ensuing
whiche thuttermost terme to then appoynted for this effect / to make
hereoff a direct answer to the cownsell /

Whereas the kinges highnes had appoynted to sir Jamys boulloyn
knight syche stuff as remayned on in the hows of blekling latelye
apperteyning to the lady off rochefort / Letter answering and
paying for the same according as itt sholde appere to be worth
the discretion of Sir richard sowthwell knight / Letters wer
deuised to s the sayde sir richarde signifying to him the kinges
highnes plesur in this behalff /

A Letter was sent vnder the stampe to for
thadmission off Elizabeth bussett in to the first rome off a sister sholde
fawle voyde / and in the meane season to receyue her / and to vse
to her all siche entreteynement partely at the kinges cost as to
charite sholde apperteyne /

A Letter was sent to Jhon Norton esquier in hamshire
aswell to deliuer to Antony James spaniard certayne specialces
and apparell fownde abowte a seruant off his murdered in
the sayde cowntye / and now coom in to the handes off the sayde
Jhon Norton as to helppe that spedie expedition off iustice
might be ministred vpon the committour off the sayde murder

Whereas sir Jhon Simpson parson off farley had accused
Jhon hownsted sergeant off the off certayne heynous
and traitorows wordes reported by the brother off the sayde Jhon
hownsted to haue ben spoken in the presence off the sayde brother
and sondry other / Any order was taken that the sayde Jhon
hownsted and his brother / Sir Jhon simpson / Jhon estend
and his wife / and Jhon lasted and his wife / whiche wer
reported to have ben present at the sayde reporte off Jhon hownstead
brother / showlde appere the tuisday next ensuing whiche shall
be the xxiiijth off february
