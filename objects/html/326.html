Certayne men off hampton / declared in the name off the
hole towne / thoccasion off theyre indigence wherebye they wer
nott able to pay the kinges highnes his fefarme as they hadde
ben accustomed / whereapon the cownsell promised to be ernest
ernest suitrers to his maieste on theyre behalff /

Whereas the mayour and commons off the towne off reading had exhibited a certayne
byll off petition to the kinges highnes aswell for thincorporation off the sayde towne
as for sondry other priuileges / the same boke being by the
kinges maiestie sent to the lordes off his cownsell / was by them
pervsed / and thinking thesame allowable / they ordeyned itt to be
deliuered to master dakers and the kinges attorney / they to
make ageyne certificat what they sholde fynde therein towching
the kinges indemnite or otherwise / And in cace they sholde so
thinke good to engrosse itt to be signed /
