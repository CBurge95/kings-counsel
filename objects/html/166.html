At Grenewhich the vjth day of
Aprill being present
Tharchebisshop of Cantorbury
The Duke of Norffolk
The Lord pryvey Seale
The gret Chambrelain of England
The gret Admirall of England
The bisshop of Duresme
The Comptroller of household
The Master of the horses
The vicechambrelain
Sir Thomas Wriothesly Secretary
Sir Rauff Sadleir knight Secretary

A lettere was written to the Treasorer of Calais
requyring him that wheras the wief of the Geoffrey
Lowdaye decessed / had Layed certain household stuff
vnto him to kepe for thassured payment of certain money
which her husband ought vnto the kinges Maieste in his lief
tyme / to reteyne the said stuff still in his hande without
selling the same / vntill he shuld haue commaundement fromhens
to the delyver it to the said wydow / And in cace he had
sold it like as the saide woman enformed the kinges highnes
he had done vnto her gret losse that then he shuld recover
on to his handes asmuch as he could get of the same again

A Lettere was sent to Master Chauncelour of thaugmentacions
signifyeng vnto him that the kinges Maieste had appointed
syr Richard Sigist prist to be one of the probendaries
at Rochester and therfor willed him to take such ordre
therin as the said prist might enioye his highnes
graunte accordingly /
