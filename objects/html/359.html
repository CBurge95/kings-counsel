Att wyndesour the first off August

The condition off this recognisance is suche as iff thaboue
bownden Erle of surrey do neyther by himselff, his seruantes
or any other at his procurement, any bodily displesur
ether by word or dede to Jhon a Ligh esquier or to any
of his then etc. or ells etc.

Att Soninghall the xiijth off
august present vt supra

Letters wer sent to sir thomas c sir thomas cheyney and
the mayour and lieutenant off the castell off dovour to signifie
vnto them that forasmoche as the kinges highnes had woon
the pere off dovour clere owt off the see / is highnes pleaser
was that theyre liberties sholde nott stretche to thesame
butt for all casualtes there chawnsing as w he had
poyntyd the medling thereof to his highnes officers there /

A Lettere was sent to sir brian tuke knight to appoynt
to all the postes betwene london and barwyke ij horsys
over and beside theyre ordinary one hors and to allow
them for the sayde iij horsys ijs by the day whiche is xijd
aboue theyre ordinarye
