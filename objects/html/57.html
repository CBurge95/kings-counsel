At wyndsour the xxiiijth day of
October beyng present
The lord Chauncelour
the lord privey Seale
The Comptroller of household
The Master of the horses
The vicechamberlayn

Vpon thexaminacion of a complaynt put vp to the lord privey seale by James
Joyner of saint albons agaynst alisander zynzam & Jakes granndo esquier
esquyres for breking of the peax & their answer vnto the sayd complaint
It was enoyned enioyned to Richard Rawnshaw
sirgeant at armes who was thought to be a gret medal in this matter & the sayd & James Joyner of St albons
that neyther they nor their wyfes nor the sonne in law
of the sayd Raynshaw shuld in any wise medle or have to do
with the body of one Katheryn Tattersall wydow whihc
is fownd by an enquest of office to be lunatik / and that also they shuld
kepe the peax against all the keing sirvantes beyng
abyers there in the towne of saint albons / It was also
enioyned to the sayd alisaunder zinzam & Jakes granndo esquires
de desquires soiournyng in the sayd Towne that
they shuld in no wise gyve occasion to any of
the sayd parties Richard Rawnshaw James Joynerd nor
their wifes or to any other of to breke the peax /
