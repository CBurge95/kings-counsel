Fowre seuerall Att westminster the xxth day off
marche being than present
The duke off Norffolk
The lorde russell
Therle off hertford
The bisshop off winchester
The lorde saint John
Sir John gage
Sir Antony wingfelde
Sir thomas wriothesly
Master dacres

Fowre seuerall commissions wer made and signed
wyth the stamp for Jhon mille Jhon Whight / and
Jhon chattrelton to take vppe vittayle for the furniture
off certeyne shippes /

A passeport was signed for robart litton repayring
to master pagett

Letteres wer written to Sir Thomas trenchard knight / thomas
trenchard and Jhon williams esquiers / to se immediate restitution
off a spanisshe pinesse and thapparail off thesame / iniustlye
taken by one Jhon bowle /

A passeport was subscribed wyth the cownsells handes
for dauid robertson skottissheman repayring in to fraunce
