At Cheynes the xxvjth of october
being present
The lorde russell high admirall off ingland
The bisshop of winchester
Sir Jhon gage knight Comptroller
Sir Antony wingfelde knight vicechawmbrelain
Sir Antony browne master of the horses
Sir thomas wriothesly knight secretary

Vpon aduertisement and depositions concerning
sondry misdemeanoures of sir Nicholas poyntz the sayde
depositions with letters wer sent to the president and
cownsail in the marches of wales to pervse and to
serche further of thesame, and likewise to certifye
and in the meane season the sayde sir Nicholas was
committed the same day to the fleete


Att windesour the xxvijth of october
being than present
The lorde russell high admiral of yngland
The bisshop of winchester
Sir Jhon gage knight Comptroller
Sir Antony wynkefelde knight vicechawmberlain
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretary

Vpon an infromation gyven that Jhon grenell
off Milcott in the cownty of warwycke sholde misvse
his owne dawghter and shote with at on of his sirvantes with
a crossebow itt was decreid that the sayde Jhon grenell
sholde be sent for tappere immediately before the cownsell
attendant vpon the kinges parson wherevpon burne
sirgeant at Armes want for him And the same day
was a Letter directid to streteley of oxford shire his
brother in law tappere likewise immediately to testyfye
agynst him

october being than present

The master of the hors
The comptroller of the howsholde
The vicechawmberlayne
Sir Thomas Wriothesley Secretarye
Sir rafe Sadlair secretarye

Thomas vaghan of the cowntye of hertford shewar off the chawmber, having bin complayned vpon
at windesour for his misdemeanour towarde Sir griffith down, and
Brockett esquier the kinges commissioneres for the subsidye , and there by
witnesse convinced of the same, and than commawndid to attnde vpon the
cownsell from place to place tyll the kinges pleser sholde be knowen
how he sholde be ponisshed therefore, after a good lesson and exhortation
by the sayde cownsell, hereafter to take no more vpon him than was fyttyng
to his degre, vpon consideration of his sorrowfull repentance for the
sayde dede, commawndyd and enioyned by the same to repayre to
Brockett esquier, and to require of him pardon and forgyvenes of
his misbehavour, was thereapon dischargid

The same day was sent to the cownsell a certificat from the com
missioners for the sewars Sowthsex, what had all redy bin don and
what was nedefull more to be doon towching the redresse off the breeche
in to pevensey marsshe

The same was deliuered to a pairsuiuant of skotland saufconduit
for the entre in to the realm of certayne skorrisshe ambassadoures, appoyntyd
to coom hither from the kinge of skottes
