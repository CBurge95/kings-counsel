A lettere was written to the duke off Suffolke to cawse a good
quantite off birtche and other wood mete for the purpose to be
felled night vnot the water syde / to be sent to barwyke with all diligence

A warrant was signed by stamp to Sir brian Tuke for the
payment off xxli to Thomas Treffrye esquier by way off reward

Doctour peter and doctour Treagonwell war appoynted to examyne
Reede late curate off honnye lane and one Cobbes /
